# Marks Distribution & Topic Weightage

> ⚠️ **Verification Caveat**
> TCS does not publicly publish section-wise marks breakdowns or per-topic question counts. The weightages below are derived from **candidate experience reports, coaching institute analyses, and placement cell data** compiled across multiple recent cycles (2022–2025). Treat these as **planning probabilities**, not guarantees. Always cross-check against your official call letter and the instructions screen at the start of your exam.

## Coding — Topic-wise Weightage

| Topic | Difficulty | Occurrence | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Arrays / Strings | Easy–Medium | Very frequent | Almost always appears |
| Math / Number Theory | Easy | Frequent | GCD, primes, factorials |
| HashMap / Hashing | Easy–Medium | Moderate | Frequency count problems |
| Sorting | Easy | Moderate | May appear as sub-task |
| Recursion / Backtracking | Medium | Moderate | Simple recursion problems |
| Stack / Queue | Medium | Occasional | Bracket matching, etc. |
| Basic DP | Medium | Occasional | Coin change, LIS type |
| Linked List | Medium | Rare | Rarely in Foundation coding |
| Matrix | Medium | Occasional | Spiral, rotation type |

**For Ninja:** Array/String + Math problems at Easy level are sufficient.  
**For Digital/Prime:** Must solve at least 1 problem fully + partial on the second; know hashmaps and basic sorting.
