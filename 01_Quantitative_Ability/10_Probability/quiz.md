# Probability — Rapid Quiz

## Q&A Pairs

Q1: P(getting 6 on one die roll)?
A1: 1/6

Q2: P(Head on fair coin)?
A2: 1/2

Q3: P(Ace from 52 cards)?
A3: 4/52 = 1/13

Q4: P(sum=7 on 2 dice)?
A4: 6/36 = 1/6

Q5: Bag: 3R 5B. P(Red)?
A5: 3/8

Q6: P(at least 1 head in 3 tosses)?
A6: 7/8 (1 - 1/8)

Q7: P(Face card from deck)?
A7: 12/52 = 3/13

Q8: P(A)=0.6, P(B)=0.5, independent. P(A and B)?
A8: 0.30

Q9: 2 dice. P(same number)?
A9: 6/36 = 1/6

Q10: P(Red card from deck)?
A10: 26/52 = 1/2

## True/False

1. P(E) + P(E') always equals 1 → **TRUE**
2. For mutually exclusive events, P(A∩B) = 0 → **TRUE**
3. P(A AND B) = P(A) + P(B) always → **FALSE** (that's OR for mutually exclusive)
4. At least 1 = 1 - P(none) → **TRUE**
5. Face cards include Aces → **FALSE** (Face cards are J, Q, K only)
