# Probability — Worked Examples

## Example 1 — Basic Dice

**Problem:** Two dice are rolled. Find P(sum = 8).

**Solution:**
- Total outcomes = 36
- Favorable: (2,6),(3,5),(4,4),(5,3),(6,2) = 5 pairs
- P(sum = 8) = 5/36

---

## Example 2 — Card Drawing

**Problem:** A card is drawn from a deck. Find P(face card or Heart).

**Solution:**
- P(Face card) = 12/52
- P(Heart) = 13/52
- P(Face card AND Heart) = 3/52 (J,Q,K of Hearts)
- P(Face OR Heart) = 12/52 + 13/52 - 3/52 = **22/52 = 11/26**

---

## Example 3 — Complementary Events

**Problem:** 3 coins tossed. P(at least one head)?

**Solution:**
- P(all tails) = (1/2)^3 = 1/8
- P(at least one head) = 1 - 1/8 = **7/8**

---

## Example 4 — Drawing Without Replacement

**Problem:** Bag has 5 red, 3 blue balls. Two drawn without replacement. P(both red)?

**Solution:**
- P(1st red) = 5/8
- P(2nd red | 1st red) = 4/7
- P(both red) = 5/8 × 4/7 = **20/56 = 5/14**

---

## Example 5 — Independent Events

**Problem:** P(passing exam A) = 0.7, P(passing exam B) = 0.6. Both independent. P(passing both)?

**Solution:**
- P(A∩B) = 0.7 × 0.6 = **0.42**
