# Probability — Practice Medium (15 Questions)

**Target:** 85%+ | **Time:** 30 minutes

### Q1
Two dice rolled. P(sum > 9)?
#### Solution
- Favorable: (4,6),(5,5),(5,6),(6,4),(6,5),(6,6) = 6
- P = 6/36 = **1/6**

### Q2
3 coins tossed. P(exactly 2 heads)?
#### Solution
- Favorable: HHT, HTH, THH = 3
- Total = 8
- P = **3/8**

### Q3
From a bag of 5 white and 3 black balls, 2 drawn without replacement. P(both white)?
#### Solution
- P = (5/8) × (4/7) = 20/56 = **5/14**

### Q4
Card drawn from a deck. P(Jack or Queen or King)?
#### Solution
- Face cards = 12; P = 12/52 = **3/13**

### Q5
P(A) = 1/3, P(B) = 1/4, independent. P(neither A nor B)?
#### Solution
- P(A') = 2/3, P(B') = 3/4
- P(neither) = 2/3 × 3/4 = **1/2**

### Q6
A and B are mutually exclusive. P(A) = 0.4, P(B) = 0.3. P(A or B)?
#### Solution
- P(A∪B) = 0.4+0.3 = **0.7**

### Q7
Die rolled twice. P(same number both times)?
#### Solution
- Favorable: (1,1),(2,2),...,(6,6) = 6
- P = 6/36 = **1/6**

### Q8
Three cards drawn from deck without replacement. P(all three are Aces)?
#### Solution
- P = (4/52) × (3/51) × (2/50) = 24/132600 = **1/5525**

### Q9
P(A) = 0.5, P(B) = 0.4, P(A∩B) = 0.2. P(A∪B)?
#### Solution
- P(A∪B) = 0.5+0.4-0.2 = **0.7**

### Q10
A student passes English with P = 3/5 and Maths with P = 2/3. P(passes both) if independent?
#### Solution
- P = 3/5 × 2/3 = **2/5**

### Q11
Bag: 6 red, 4 blue. Draw 2 with replacement. P(both red)?
#### Solution
- P = (6/10) × (6/10) = 36/100 = **9/25**

### Q12
P(sum of 2 dice = 4)?
#### Solution
- Favorable: (1,3),(2,2),(3,1) = 3
- P = 3/36 = **1/12**

### Q13
4 coins tossed. P(at least 1 tail)?
#### Solution
- P(no tail) = (1/2)^4 = 1/16
- P(at least 1) = 1-1/16 = **15/16**

### Q14
From 52 cards, 2 drawn. P(1 king, 1 queen)?
#### Solution
- 4C1 × 4C1 / 52C2 = 16/1326 = **8/663**

### Q15
P(A) = 1/2, P(A∩B) = 1/6. Find P(B|A)?
#### Solution
- P(B|A) = P(A∩B)/P(A) = (1/6)/(1/2) = **1/3**
