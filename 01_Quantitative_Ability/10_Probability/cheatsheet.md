# Probability — Cheatsheet

**P(E) = Favorable / Total | P(E') = 1 - P(E)**

| Rule | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Addition (general) | P(A)+P(B)-P(A∩B) |
| Independent AND | P(A)×P(B) |
| Conditional | P(A∩B)/P(A) |
| At least 1 | 1 - P(none) |

## Sample Spaces
| Setup | Total |
|-------|-------|
| n coins | 2^n |
| n dice | 6^n |
| Standard deck | 52 |

## Card Quick Reference
- Red = 26, Black = 26
- Each suit = 13
- Face cards (J,Q,K) = 12
- Aces = 4
- P(Ace) = 1/13, P(Face) = 3/13

## Two Dice Sums
- Sum 7: P = 1/6 (most likely)
- Sum 2 or 12: P = 1/36 (least likely)
