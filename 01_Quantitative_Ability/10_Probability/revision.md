# Probability — Quick Revision

## Core Formula
$$P(E) = \frac{\text{Favorable}}{\text{Total}}$$

## Key Rules
| Rule | Formula |
|------|---------|
| Complement | P(E') = 1 - P(E) |
| Addition | P(A∪B) = P(A)+P(B)-P(A∩B) |
| Mutual exclusion | P(A∪B) = P(A)+P(B) |
| Independent AND | P(A∩B) = P(A)×P(B) |
| Conditional | P(B\|A) = P(A∩B)/P(A) |

## Standard Sizes
- 1 die: 6, 2 dice: 36
- 1 coin: 2, n coins: 2^n
- Deck: 52 (Red=26, Face=12, Aces=4)

## Key Shortcuts
- At least 1 = 1 - P(none)
- Sum 7 on two dice: P = 6/36 = 1/6 (most likely)
- P(Ace from deck) = 1/13
