# Probability — Previous Year Questions

### PYQ1 (TCS 2023)
From 52 cards, one drawn. P(Queen of hearts)?
- Only 1 such card → **1/52**

### PYQ2 (TCS 2023)
Two dice. P(sum ≤ 4)?
- Sum 2:(1,1)=1; Sum 3:(1,2)(2,1)=2; Sum 4:(1,3)(2,2)(3,1)=3; Total=6
- **6/36 = 1/6**

### PYQ3 (TCS 2022)
3 coins. P(at least 1 head)?
- 1-(1/2)^3 = **7/8**

### PYQ4 (TCS 2022)
Bag: 3 red, 4 blue, 5 green. P(picking green or blue)?
- 4+5=9; P = **9/12 = 3/4**

### PYQ5 (TCS 2022)
P(A) = 2/5, P(B) = 1/3, independent. P(A and B)?
- 2/5 × 1/3 = **2/15**

### PYQ6 (TCS 2021)
Cards: P(red face card)?
- Red face cards: 3 hearts + 3 diamonds = 6; **6/52 = 3/26**

### PYQ7 (TCS 2021)
Two dice. P(product = 12)?
- (2,6)(3,4)(4,3)(6,2) = 4; **4/36 = 1/9**

### PYQ8 (TCS 2021)
Coin tossed 4 times. P(exactly 2 tails)?
- 4C2 × (1/2)^4 = 6/16 = **3/8**

### PYQ9 (TCS 2023)
Bag: 5W, 4B, 3R. P(not white)?
- 4+3=7; **7/12**

### PYQ10 (TCS 2022)
P(A) = 0.3, P(A or B) = 0.7, P(A and B) = 0.15. Find P(B)?
- 0.7 = 0.3 + P(B) - 0.15 → **P(B) = 0.55**

### PYQ11 (TCS 2023)
2 cards from deck (no replacement). P(1st ace, 2nd ace)?
- (4/52)(3/51) = 12/2652 = **1/221**

### PYQ12 (TCS 2022)
Die: P(not 3 or 5)?
- Favorable: 1,2,4,6 = 4; **4/6 = 2/3**

### PYQ13 (TCS 2021)
3 fair coins. P(all same)?
- HHH, TTT = 2; **2/8 = 1/4**

### PYQ14 (TCS 2023)
From 10 balls (6 red, 4 blue), pick 3. P(exactly 2 red)?
- 6C2 × 4C1 / 10C3 = 15×4/120 = **60/120 = 1/2**

### PYQ15 (TCS 2022)
P(at least one 6 in 3 dice rolls)?
- 1-(5/6)^3 = 1-125/216 = **91/216**
