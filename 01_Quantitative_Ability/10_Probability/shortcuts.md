## 3. Card Drawing Shortcuts
- P(Ace) = 4/52 = 1/13
- P(Face card) = 12/52 = 3/13
- P(Red card) = 26/52 = 1/2
- P(Heart) = 13/52 = 1/4

## 4. Coin Toss Shortcuts
- P(All heads in n tosses) = (1/2)^n
- P(At least 1 head) = 1 - (1/2)^n

## 5. Without Replacement — Adjust Denominator
First draw: n items in bag → denominator = n
Second draw (without replacement): denominator = n-1
