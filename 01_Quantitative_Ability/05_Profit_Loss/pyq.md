### PYQ1 — Dishonest Dealer Compound
A merchant sells sugar at a profit of $20\%$ on Cost Price, but he uses a false weight of 800g instead of 1kg. What is his overall profit percentage?
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 90s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Standard Solution:**
    *   Let CP of 1000g be 100 Rupees. SP = 120 Rupees.
    *   He only delivers 800g, which cost him 80 Rupees.
    *   Overall profit % = $rac{120 - 80}{80} 	imes 100 = 50\%$.
*   **Faster Method:**
    *   Profit multiplier $= 1.20 	imes rac{1000}{800} = 1.20 	imes 1.25 = 1.50 ightarrow 50\%$ profit.

### PYQ3 — Markup discount equation
By what percentage should a dealer mark up his goods costing 450 Rupees so that he can offer a $10\%$ discount and still earn a $26\%$ profit?
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 90s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Standard Solution:**
    *   $$rac{MP}{CP} = rac{100 + 26}{100 - 10} = rac{126}{90} = 1.40$$.
    *   Markup = $40\%$.
*   **Faster Method:**
    *   Since $rac{MP}{CP} = 1.40$, markup is $40\%$ (independent of actual cost).

### PYQ4 — Successive discounts three
Find the single equivalent discount of three successive discounts of $10\%$, $15\%$, and $20\%$.

*   **Standard Solution:**
    *   Multiplier = $0.90 	imes 0.85 	imes 0.80 = 0.72 	imes 0.85 = 0.612$.

### PYQ14 — Excluded element profit
A dealer buys 100 kg of rice. He sells 40 kg at a $10\%$ profit and the rest at a $20\%$ profit. What is his overall profit percentage?
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 45s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Standard Solution:**
    *   Let CP be 1 Rupee/kg. Total CP = 100.
    *   SP of 40 kg = $40 	imes 1.10 = 44$.
    *   SP of 60 kg = $60 	imes 1.20 = 72$.
    *   Total SP = $44 + 72 = 116 \implies 16\%$ profit.
*   **Faster Method:**
    *   Weighted average = $rac{40(10) + 60(20)}{100} = rac{400 + 1200}{100} = 16\%$.
