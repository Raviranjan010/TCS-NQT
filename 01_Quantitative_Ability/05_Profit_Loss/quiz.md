## Part 2 — 5 True/False Statements

### 1. Statement
Discount is always calculated on the Cost Price of the goods.
*   **Answer:** **FALSE**
*   **Explanation:** Discount is strictly calculated on the Marked Price (MP).

### 2. Statement
If two items are bought for the same Cost Price and sold such that one gains $15\%$ and the other loses $15\%$, there is no net profit or loss.
*   **Answer:** **TRUE**
*   **Explanation:** Net profit/loss is the average of the two margins when CPs are equal: $rac{15\% - 15\%}{2} = 0\%$.

### 3. Statement
The profit percentage can exceed the markup percentage if a discount is offered.
*   **Answer:** **FALSE**
*   **Explanation:** Offering a discount always reduces the profit percentage below the markup percentage.
