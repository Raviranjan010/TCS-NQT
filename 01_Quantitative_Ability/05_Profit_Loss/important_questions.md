### Q9

*   **Difficulty:** Hard | **Expected Time:** 120s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   SP per lemon = $40/45 = 8/9$ Rupees.
    *   Since loss is $20\%$, CP = $rac{8/9}{0.80} = 10/9$ Rupees.
