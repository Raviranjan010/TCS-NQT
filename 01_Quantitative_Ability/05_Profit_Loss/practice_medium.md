### Q1
A shopkeeper marks up his goods by $30\%$ and then offers a discount of $10\%$. Find his net profit percentage.

#### Solution
1.  Let Cost Price ($CP$) = 100 Rupees.
2.  Marked Price ($MP$) = $100 	imes 1.30 = 130$ Rupees.
3.  Selling Price ($SP$) after $10\%$ discount = $130 	imes 0.90 = 117$ Rupees.
4.  Profit Percentage = $rac{117 - 100}{100} 	imes 100 = 17\%$.

### Q3
By selling 33 meters of cloth, a merchant gains the Selling Price of 11 meters of cloth. Find his gain percentage.

#### Solution
1.  Let the Selling Price of 1 meter of cloth be 1 Rupee.
2.  Total SP (33 meters) = 33 Rupees.
3.  Gain = SP of 11 meters = 11 Rupees.
4.  Cost Price ($CP$) = $SP - 	ext{Gain} = 33 - 11 = 22$ Rupees.
5.  $$	ext{Gain \%} = rac{	ext{Gain}}{CP} 	imes 100 = rac{11}{22} 	imes 100 = 50\%$$

### Q6
Find the single equivalent discount of three successive discounts of $10\%$, $20\%$, and $25\%$.

#### Solution
1.  Let MP = 100.
2.  After $10\%$ discount: Price = 90.
3.  After $20\%$ discount: Price = $90 	imes 0.80 = 72$.
4.  After $25\%$ discount: Price = $72 	imes 0.75 = 54$.
5.  Single equivalent discount = $100 - 54 = 46\%$.

### Q8

#### Solution
