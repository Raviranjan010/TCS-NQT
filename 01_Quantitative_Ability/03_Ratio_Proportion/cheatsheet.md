# Ratio & Proportion — Cheatsheet

This cheatsheet compiles the most critical formulas and shortcuts onto a single screen for quick reference.

---

## 1. Proportionality Terms

*   **Proportion Equation:** $a:b :: c:d \implies ad = bc$
*   **Mean Proportional:** $b = \sqrt{ac}$
*   **Third Proportional:** $c = b^2 / a$
*   **Fourth Proportional:** $d = bc / a$

---

## 2. Partnerships & Profit Sharing

$$\text{Profit Ratio} = (I_A \times T_A) : (I_B \times T_B) : (I_C \times T_C)$$
*   **Active Partner:** Deduct salary from total profit first, then distribute remainder.

---

## 3. Alligation & Replacements

### Alligation Cross Formula
$$\frac{\text{Cheaper Quantity } (q_c)}{\text{Dearer Quantity } (q_d)} = \frac{D - M}{M - C}$$

### Successive Dilution Formula
$$Q_n = Q_0 \times \left(1 - \frac{x}{V}\right)^n$$
*   Track the concentration of the **original pure liquid**, not the water/added liquid.

---

## 4. Balancing Shortcut (N-Method)

Combine $A:B = x:y$ and $B:C = z:w$:
$$A:B:C = (x \cdot z) : (y \cdot z) : (y \cdot w)$$
