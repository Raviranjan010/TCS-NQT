# Ratio & Proportion — Rapid Quiz (10 Q&As + 5 True/False)

Test your conceptual understanding and speed with this quick self-assessment.

## Part 1 — 10 Rapid Q&A Pairs

### Q1
If $A:B = 2:3$ and $B:C = 3:5$, find $A:C$.
*   **Answer:** $2:5$
*   **Explanation:** $B$ is already balanced at 3, so $A:B:C = 2:3:5 \implies A:C = 2:5$.

### Q2
What is the duplicate ratio of $3:4$?
*   **Answer:** $9:16$
*   **Explanation:** Square both parts: $3^2 : 4^2 = 9:16$.

### Q3
Find the mean proportional between 9 and 25.
*   **Answer:** 15
*   **Explanation:** $\sqrt{9 \times 25} = \sqrt{225} = 15$.

### Q4
Find the third proportional to 4 and 6.
*   **Answer:** 9
*   **Explanation:** $c = b^2 / a = 36 / 4 = 9$.

### Q5
What is the fourth proportional to 2, 5, and 8?
*   **Answer:** 20
*   **Explanation:** $d = \frac{5 \times 8}{2} = 20$.

### Q6
If A and B invest capital in ratio 3:4 for equal time, what is their profit ratio?
*   **Answer:** $3:4$
*   **Explanation:** Since time is equal, profit ratio equals investment ratio.

### Q7
In what ratio must milk at 30 Rupees/L be mixed with water (0 Rupees/L) to get a mixture worth 20 Rupees/L?
*   **Answer:** $2:1$ (Milk : Water)
*   **Explanation:** $\text{Ratio} = \frac{20 - 0}{30 - 20} = \frac{20}{10} = 2:1$.

### Q8
If $x:y = 1:2$, find $(3x+y):(x+2y)$.
*   **Answer:** $5:5$ (or $1:1$)
*   **Explanation:** Substitute $x=1, y=2 \rightarrow \frac{3(1) + 2}{1 + 2(2)} = \frac{5}{5} = 1:1$.

### Q9
A gold bar breaks into parts weighing 1:2. If value $\propto \text{weight}^2$, find value reduction fraction.
*   **Answer:** $\frac{4}{9}$ loss (or $44.44\%$ loss)
*   **Explanation:** Initial = $3^2 = 9$. Broken = $1^2 + 2^2 = 5$. Loss = $4/9$.

### Q10
If 5 men build a wall in 10 days, how many days do 10 men take?
*   **Answer:** 5 days
*   **Explanation:** $5 \times 10 = 10 \times D_2 \implies D_2 = 5$.

## Part 2 — 5 True/False Statements

### 1. Statement
If $a, b, c, d$ are in proportion, then the product of extremes equals the product of means.
*   **Answer:** **TRUE**
*   **Explanation:** $a:b :: c:d \implies ad = bc$.

### 2. Statement
The alligation rule can be applied even if the three cost prices are of different units.
*   **Answer:** **FALSE**
*   **Explanation:** All three quantities must represent the same variable in the same unit.

### 3. Statement
In a compound partnership, the profit ratio is independent of the investment durations.
*   **Answer:** **FALSE**
*   **Explanation:** Profit ratio depends on the product of investment and active time.

### 4. Statement
If $x$ varies inversely with $y$, then doubling $x$ halves $y$.
*   **Answer:** **TRUE**
*   **Explanation:** $x \cdot y = k \implies (2x) \cdot (y/2) = k$.

### 5. Statement
The third proportional to $a$ and $b$ is equal to the fourth proportional to $a, b,$ and $b$.
*   **Answer:** **TRUE**
*   **Explanation:** Third proportional $c = b^2/a$. Fourth proportional to $a, b, b$ is $d = (b \cdot b)/a = b^2/a$.
