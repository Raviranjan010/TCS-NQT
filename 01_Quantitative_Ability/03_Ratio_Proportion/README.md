# Ratio & Proportion - Module Overview

This module covers the core principles of Ratio, Proportion, Partnership, and Mixture & Alligation. These concepts are foundational for TCS NQT, appearing in direct word problems and as auxiliary tools for solving Time & Work, Speed, and Interest questions.

---

## Metadata

*   **TCS NQT Frequency:** `🔴 High` (Appears as 2-3 direct questions and in DI sets)
*   **Difficulty Level:** `🟢 Easy` to `🟡 Medium`
*   **Core Pre-requisites:** Basic Arithmetic operations, Equation solving

---

## Syllabus Coverage

1.  **Ratios:** Meaning, comparison, properties, and composition of ratios.
2.  **Proportions:** Direct, inverse, and continued proportion. Mean, third, and fourth proportionals.
3.  **Partnership:** Simple and compound partnerships, profit-sharing ratios, active vs. sleeping partners.
4.  **Mixture & Alligation:** The rule of alligation, mixing two ingredients, replacement formulas, and multi-step dilution.
