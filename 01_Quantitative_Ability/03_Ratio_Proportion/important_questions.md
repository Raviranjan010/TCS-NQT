# Ratio & Proportion — Top 20 Most Repeated TCS-Style Questions

*   **TCS NQT Target:** Detailed coverage of recurring quantitative trends.
*   **Assessment Format:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ) & Fill-in-the-Blanks (FIB).

### Q1
If $A:B = 5:7$ and $B:C = 6:11$, find $A:B:C$.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 30s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   Using the N-method:
        *   $A = 5 \times 6 = 30$
        *   $B = 7 \times 6 = 42$
        *   $C = 7 \times 11 = 77$
    *   Ratio = $30:42:77$.

---

### Q2
A sum of money is divided among A, B, and C in the ratio $2:3:4$. If A's share is 500 Rupees, find the total sum.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 30s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   $A = 2x = 500 \implies x = 250$.
    *   Total sum = $(2 + 3 + 4)x = 9x = 9 \times 250 = 2250$ Rupees.

---

### Q3
The ratio of ages of two brothers is $3:4$. Five years ago, their ratio was $2:3$. Find their present ages.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 45s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   Present ages: $3x, 4x$.
    *   $$\frac{3x - 5}{4x - 5} = \frac{2}{3} \implies 9x - 15 = 8x - 10 \implies x = 5$$.
    *   Ages are $15$ and $20$ years.

### Q4
Find the mean proportional of 0.08 and 0.18.
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 60s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   $$\text{Mean} = \sqrt{0.08 \times 0.18} = \sqrt{\frac{8}{100} \times \frac{18}{100}} = \sqrt{\frac{144}{10000}} = \frac{12}{100} = 0.12$$.

### Q5
What is the third proportional to 16 and 36?
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 45s | **TCS Frequency:** Medium
*   **Solution:**
    *   $$c = \frac{b^2}{a} = \frac{36 \times 36}{16} = \frac{1296}{16} = 81$$.

### Q6
Find the fourth proportional to 4, 9, and 12.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 30s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   $$d = \frac{bc}{a} = \frac{9 \times 12}{4} = 27$$.

### Q7
A bag contains 1 Rupee, 50p, and 25p coins in the ratio $5:6:8$. If the total money is 240 Rupees, find the number of 25p coins.
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 90s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   Values of coins: $1x, 0.5x, 0.25x$.
    *   Coin counts: $5x, 6x, 8x$.
    *   $5x(1) + 6x(0.5) + 8x(0.25) = 240 \implies 5x + 3x + 2x = 240 \implies 10x = 240 \implies x = 24$.
    *   25p coins = $8 \times 24 = 192$.

### Q8
If $x:y = 3:4$, find the value of $(7x+3y):(7x-3y)$.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 45s | **TCS Frequency:** Medium
*   **Solution:**
    *   Substitute $x=3, y=4 \rightarrow \frac{7(3) + 3(4)}{7(3) - 3(4)} = \frac{21+12}{21-12} = \frac{33}{9} = \frac{11}{3} \rightarrow 11:3$.

### Q9
A and B start a business with investments of 5000 Rupees and 4500 Rupees respectively. After 4 months, A takes out half of his capital. After 2 more months, C enters with 7000 Rupees. Find their profit sharing ratio at the end of 12 months.
*   **Difficulty:** Hard | **Expected Time:** 150s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   A's share: $(5000 \times 4) + (2500 \times 8) = 20000 + 20000 = 40000$.
    *   B's share: $4500 \times 12 = 54000$.
    *   C's share: $7000 \times 6 = 42000$ (C enters at 6 months, remaining time = 6 months).
    *   Ratio $A:B:C = 40000 : 54000 : 42000 = 20 : 27 : 21$.

### Q10
In a mixture of 45 liters, the ratio of milk and water is $4:1$. How much water must be added to make it $3:2$?
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 90s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   Initial: Milk = 36, Water = 9.
    *   Let water added be $x$.
    *   $$\frac{36}{9 + x} = \frac{3}{2} \implies 72 = 27 + 3x \implies 3x = 45 \implies x = 15 \text{ liters}$$.

### Q11
If 10% of $x$ is equal to 15% of $y$ and 20% of $z$, find the ratio $x:y:z$.
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 60s | **TCS Frequency:** Medium
*   **Solution:**
    *   $0.10x = 0.15y = 0.20z \implies 2x = 3y = 4z$.
    *   Find LCM of 2, 3, 4 = 12.
    *   $x = 6, y = 4, z = 3 \implies 6:4:3$.

### Q12
Two numbers are in the ratio $2:3$. If 4 is subtracted from each, the ratio becomes $3:5$. Find the numbers.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 45s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   $$\frac{2x - 4}{3x - 4} = \frac{3}{5} \implies 10x - 20 = 9x - 12 \implies x = 8$$.
    *   Numbers are $16$ and $24$.

### Q13
A sum of 1200 Rupees is divided among A, B, and C such that A's share is twice B's share and B's share is thrice C's share. Find B's share.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 45s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   Let C = $x$, B = $3x$, A = $2(3x) = 6x$.
    *   $6x + 3x + x = 1200 \implies 10x = 1200 \implies x = 120$.
    *   B's share = $3x = 360$ Rupees.

---

### Q14
The incomes of A and B are in the ratio $3:2$ and their expenditures are in the ratio $5:3$. If A saves 1000 Rupees and B saves 900 Rupees, find their incomes.
*   **Difficulty:** Hard | **Expected Time:** 120s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   Let incomes be $3x, 2x$.
    *   $$\frac{3x - 1000}{2x - 900} = \frac{5}{3} \implies 9x - 3000 = 10x - 4500 \implies x = 1500$$.
    *   Incomes: A = 4500, B = 3000 Rupees.

### Q15
An alloy contains zinc and copper in the ratio $5:3$ and another alloy contains them in the ratio $8:5$. If equal weights are melted together, find the ratio in the new alloy.
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 90s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   Fraction in Alloy 1: Zinc = $5/8$, Copper = $3/8$.
    *   Fraction in Alloy 2: Zinc = $8/13$, Copper = $5/13$.
    *   New Zinc = $\frac{5}{8} + \frac{8}{13} = \frac{65 + 64}{104} = \frac{129}{104}$.
    *   New Copper = $\frac{3}{8} + \frac{5}{13} = \frac{39 + 40}{104} = \frac{79}{104}$.
    *   Ratio = $129:79$.

### Q16
If $\frac{A}{3} = \frac{B}{4} = \frac{C}{5}$, find $A:B:C$.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 15s | **TCS Frequency:** Low
*   **Solution:**
    *   Let the terms be equal to $k \implies A=3k, B=4k, C=5k \implies 3:4:5$.

### Q17
A mixture of 60 liters contains milk and water in the ratio $2:1$. If 15 liters of water is added, find the new ratio.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 45s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   Milk = 40, Water = 20.
    *   New water = 35. New ratio = $40:35 = 8:7$.

### Q18
What number must be added to each of the numbers 6, 15, 20, 43 to make them proportional?
*   **Difficulty:** Hard | **Expected Time:** 120s | **TCS Frequency:** Medium
*   **Solution:**
    *   $$\frac{6 + x}{15 + x} = \frac{20 + x}{43 + x} \implies (6+x)(43+x) = (15+x)(20+x)$$
    *   $$258 + 49x + x^2 = 300 + 35x + x^2 \implies 14x = 42 \implies x = 3$$.

---

### Q19
The speed of three cars are in the ratio $2:3:4$. What is the ratio of time taken by them to cover the same distance?
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 60s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   Time ratio = $\frac{1}{2} : \frac{1}{3} : \frac{1}{4} = 6 : 4 : 3$ (multiplying by LCM 12).

### Q20
If the ratio of A's salary to B's is $4:5$ and B's to C's is $2:3$. If A's salary is 800 Rupees, find C's salary.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 45s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   Balance ratio $A:B:C = 8:10:15$.
    *   $8x = 800 \implies x = 100$.
    *   C's salary = $15x = 1500$ Rupees.
