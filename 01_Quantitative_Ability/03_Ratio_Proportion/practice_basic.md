# Ratio & Proportion — Practice Basic (15 Questions)

This practice set contains 15 basic questions covering ratio conversions, continuous proportions, and simple partnerships.

*   **Suggested Time:** 15 minutes (60 seconds per question)
*   **Target Accuracy:** 100%

---

### Q1
Find the compound ratio of $2:3$ and $4:5$.
*   **Answer:** $8:15$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Compound Ratio} = \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{4}{5} = \frac{8}{15} \rightarrow 8:15$$

### Q2
If $A:B = 3:4$ and $B:C = 8:9$, find $A:C$.
*   **Answer:** $2:3$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\frac{A}{C} = \frac{A}{B} \times \frac{B}{C} = \frac{3}{4} \times \frac{8}{9} = \frac{24}{36} = \frac{2}{3}$$

### Q3
If $A:B = 2:3$ and $B:C = 4:5$, find $A:B:C$.
*   **Answer:** $8:12:15$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    Multiply $A = 2 \times 4 = 8$; $B = 3 \times 4 = 12$; $C = 3 \times 5 = 15$.

### Q4
Find the mean proportional between 4 and 16.
*   **Answer:** 8
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Mean Proportional} = \sqrt{4 \times 16} = \sqrt{64} = 8$$

### Q5
Find the third proportional to 9 and 12.
*   **Answer:** 16
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Third Proportional} = \frac{12^2}{9} = \frac{144}{9} = 16$$

### Q6
Find the fourth proportional to 3, 5, and 21.
*   **Answer:** 35
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Fourth Proportional} = \frac{5 \times 21}{3} = 5 \times 7 = 35$$

### Q7
If $\frac{x}{y} = \frac{3}{4}$, find the value of $\frac{2x+3y}{3x+4y}$.
*   **Answer:** $\frac{18}{25}$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    Substitute $x=3, y=4 \rightarrow \frac{2(3) + 3(4)}{3(3) + 4(4)} = \frac{6+12}{9+16} = \frac{18}{25}$.

### Q8
Divide 1200 Rupees between A and B in the ratio $5:7$. Find A's share.
*   **Answer:** 500 Rupees
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{A's share} = \frac{5}{5+7} \times 1200 = \frac{5}{12} \times 1200 = 500 \text{ Rupees}$$

### Q9
Two numbers are in the ratio $3:5$. If their sum is 80, find the smaller number.
*   **Answer:** 30
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Smaller number} = \frac{3}{8} \times 80 = 30$$

### Q10
Three numbers are in the ratio $1:2:3$. If their sum is 180, find the largest number.
*   **Answer:** 90
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Largest number} = \frac{3}{6} \times 180 = 90$$

### Q11
If $A$ and $B$ start a business investing 20,000 and 30,000 Rupees respectively for 1 year, what is their profit sharing ratio?
*   **Answer:** $2:3$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Ratio} = 20,000 : 30,000 = 2 : 3$$

### Q12
If 15 men can do a work in 20 days, how many men are needed to do the work in 10 days?
*   **Answer:** 30 men
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    Since work is constant: $M_1 D_1 = M_2 D_2 \rightarrow 15 \times 20 = M_2 \times 10 \rightarrow M_2 = 30$.

### Q13
If a car covers a distance in 4 hours at 60 km/h, how much time will it take at 80 km/h?
*   **Answer:** 3 hours
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    Since distance is constant: $S_1 T_1 = S_2 T_2 \rightarrow 60 \times 4 = 80 \times T_2 \rightarrow T_2 = 3$.

### Q14
Find the duplicate ratio of $5:6$.
*   **Answer:** $25:36$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Duplicate Ratio} = 5^2 : 6^2 = 25 : 36$$

### Q15
What is the triplicate ratio of $2:3$?
*   **Answer:** $8:27$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Triplicate Ratio} = 2^3 : 3^3 = 8 : 27$$
