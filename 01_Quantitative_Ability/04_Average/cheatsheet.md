# Average — Cheatsheet

This cheatsheet compiles the most critical formulas and shortcuts onto a single screen for quick reference.

---

## 1. Core Equations

*   **Arithmetic Mean:** $A = \text{Sum} / N$
*   **Weighted Average:** $A_w = \frac{n_1 A_1 + n_2 A_2 + \dots}{n_1 + n_2 + \dots}$
    *   *Tip:* Simplify counts ($n_i$) into their simplest ratio before multiplying.

---

## 2. Average Speed

*   **Equal segments (2 speeds):** $v_{\text{avg}} = \frac{2v_1v_2}{v_1+v_2}$
*   **Equal segments (3 speeds):** $v_{\text{avg}} = \frac{3v_1v_2v_3}{v_1v_2 + v_2v_3 + v_3v_1}$
*   **Equal time intervals:** $v_{\text{avg}} = \frac{v_1+v_2}{2}$

---

## 3. Deviation Method (Group Changes)

*   **Addition:** $\text{New Value} = \text{New Average} + N \times (\text{Average Increase})$
    *   *where $N$ is the count of the initial group.*
*   **Removal:** $\text{Removed Value} = \text{Old Average} + (N-1) \times (\text{Average Decrease})$
    *   *where $N$ is the count of the initial group.*
*   **Substitution:** $\text{New Value} = \text{Replaced Value} + N \times (\text{Average Increase})$
    *   *where $N$ is the count of the group.*

---

## 4. AP Series Midpoint

For consecutive numbers, consecutive evens, or consecutive odds:
*   $\text{Average} = \frac{\text{First Term} + \text{Last Term}}{2}$
*   The average is the exact middle term.
