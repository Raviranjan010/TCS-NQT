# Time & Work - Module Overview

This module covers the mathematical principles of Time & Work, rate-efficiency relations, pipes & cisterns, and combined work involving leaks (negative work).

---

## Metadata

*   **TCS NQT Frequency:** `🔴 High` (Appears as 2 direct word problems per exam cycle)
*   **Difficulty Level:** `🟢 Easy` to `🟡 Medium`
*   **Core Pre-requisites:** Fractions, LCM, and Ratios

---

## Study Guide & Syllabus

1.  **Rate-Efficiency Principle:** Work done is the product of rate (efficiency) and duration: $W = R 	imes T$.
2.  **LCM Method:** Solving time-based problems by assuming total work is the LCM of given days.
3.  **Pipes & Cisterns:** Multi-inlet and outlet flow systems.
4.  **Combined Work with Leaks:** Adding and subtracting flow rates (negative work by outlets/leaks).
