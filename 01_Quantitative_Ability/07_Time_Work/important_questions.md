### Q3
A is thrice as efficient as B and takes 10 days less than B. Find the time taken by B.
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 45s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   $T_B - T_A = 2x = 10 \implies x = 5 \implies T_B = 15$ days.

### Q4

*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 20s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   $$10 	imes 15 = 15 	imes D_2 \implies D_2 = 10 	ext{ days}$$.

### Q5
An inlet fills a pool in 6 hours and a leak empties it in 12 hours. Find the net time to fill.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 30s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   $$	ext{Time} = rac{6 	imes 12}{12 - 6} = 12 	ext{ hours}$$.

### Q6
A and B can do a work in 12 days, B and C in 15 days, and C and A in 20 days. Find combined days.
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 60s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   LCM $= 60$. Combined efficiency $= (5 + 4 + 3) / 2 = 6 \implies 10$ days.

### Q7
A, B, and C do a work in 10, 15, and 30 days. If A is assisted by B and C on alternate days, find total days.
*   **Difficulty:** Hard | **Expected Time:** 120s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   LCM $= 30$. Efficiencies: A=3, B=2, C=1.
    *   Day 1 (A+B) $= 5$ units. Day 2 (A+C) $= 4$ units.
    *   Work in 2 days $= 9$ units.
    *   In 6 days $= 27$ units. Remaining $= 3$ units.
    *   Day 7 (A+B turn): completes remaining 3 units in $3/5$ day $ightarrow 6.6$ days.

### Q8
If 5 men or 9 women can do a work in 19 days, in how many days can 3 men and 6 women do it?
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 90s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   $5M = 9W \implies 3M = 5.4W$.
    *   $3M + 6W = 11.4W$.
    *   $$9 	imes 19 = 11.4 	imes D \implies D = 15 	ext{ days}$$.

### Q9
A can do a work in 15 days. He works for 5 days and leaves. B completes the rest in 20 days. Find B's alone time.
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 60s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   A completes $5/15 = 1/3$ work. Remaining $2/3$ done by B in 20 days.
    *   B alone takes $20 	imes rac{3}{2} = 30$ days.

### Q10
A and B earn 600 Rupees for a job. A alone can do it in 6 days, B in 8 days. With C, they do it in 3 days. Find C's share.
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 60s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   LCM $= 24$. Efficiencies: A = 4, B = 3, A+B+C = 8 $\implies$ C = 1.
    *   C's share $= 1/8 	imes 600 = 75$ Rupees.

### Q11
A leak can empty a tank in 10 hours. An inlet fills 4 L/min. When both open, tank empties in 15 hours. Find capacity.
*   **Difficulty:** Hard | **Expected Time:** 120s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   LCM $= 30$. Leak rate $= -3$, Net rate $= -2 \implies$ Inlet $= +1$ unit/hour.
    *   Filling time $= 30$ hours.
    *   Capacity $= 30 	imes 60 	imes 4 = 7200$ liters.

### Q13
If A takes 8 days and B takes 16 days, find alternate days work starting with A.
*   **Answer:** $10.5$ days (approx)
*   **Solution:**
    *   LCM $= 16$. Efficiencies: A=2, B=1.
    *   2-day cycle $= 3$ units. In 10 days $= 15$ units. Remaining $= 1$ unit.
    *   Day 11 (A turn) takes $1/2$ day $ightarrow 10.5$ days.

### Q14
A and B together do a work in 30 days. They worked for 20 days and B left. A completes the rest in 20 days. Find A alone.
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 90s | **TCS Frequency:** Medium
*   **Solution:**
    *   $2/3$ work done in 20 days. Remaining $1/3$ done by A in 20 days $\implies$ A alone $= 60$ days.

### Q15
If 8 men can reap a field in 6 days, how many men are needed to reap it in 4 days?
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 20s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   $8 	imes 6 = M_2 	imes 4 \implies M_2 = 12$ men.

### Q16
An inlet fills a pool in 8 hours and B fills in 12 hours. Outlet C empties it in 6 hours. Find time if all open.
*   **Difficulty:** Medium | **Expected Time:** 60s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   LCM $= 24$. Rates: A = +3, B = +2, C = -4.
    *   Net rate $= +1$ unit/hour $\implies 24$ hours.

### Q17
A can do a work in 12 days. B is $60\%$ more efficient than A. Find B's time.
*   **Difficulty:** Easy | **Expected Time:** 30s | **TCS Frequency:** High
*   **Solution:**
    *   $T_B = 12 / 1.6 = 7.5$ days.

### Q18
If A can do a work in 10 days and B in 15 days, and B leaves 2 days before completion, find total days.
*   **Difficulty:** Hard | **Expected Time:** 120s | **TCS Frequency:** Medium
*   **Solution:**
    *   LCM $= 30$. A = 3, B = 2.
    *   Let total days be $x \implies 3x + 2(x-2) = 30 \implies 5x = 34 \implies x = 6.8$ days.

### Q19

*   **Solution:**
