### Q7
If A is twice as efficient as B, and B takes 20 days to complete a task, how many days will A take?
*   **Answer:** 10 days
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    Since A is twice as efficient, A takes half the time of B: $20 / 2 = 10$ days.

### Q9
