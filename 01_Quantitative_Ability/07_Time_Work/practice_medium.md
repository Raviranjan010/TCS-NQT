### Q1
A and B can complete a work in 15 days and 10 days respectively. They began the work together but A left after 2 days. In how many days was the remaining work completed by B?

#### Solution
1.  Assume Total Work = LCM of 15 and 10 = 30 units.
2.  Efficiencies: A = 2 units/day, B = 3 units/day.
3.  Combined efficiency = 5 units/day.
4.  Work completed in first 2 days = $5 	imes 2 = 10$ units.
5.  Remaining work = $30 - 10 = 20$ units.
6.  Time taken by B to complete remaining work = $20 / 3 = 6rac{2}{3}$ days.

### Q2
A, B, and C can do a piece of work in 24, 30, and 40 days respectively. They began the work together but C left 4 days before the completion of the work. In how many days was the work completed?

#### Solution
1.  Assume Total Work = LCM of 24, 30, and 40 = 120 units.
2.  Efficiencies: A = 5, B = 4, C = 3 units/day.
3.  Let the total time taken to complete the work be $x$ days.
    *   A works for $x$ days $ightarrow$ Work done = $5x$.
    *   B works for $x$ days $ightarrow$ Work done = $4x$.
    *   C works for $x - 4$ days $ightarrow$ Work done = $3(x - 4)$.
4.  Total Work Equation:
    $$5x + 4x + 3(x - 4) = 120$$
    $$12x - 12 = 120 \implies 12x = 132 \implies x = 11 	ext{ days}$$

### Q4
A cistern has two inlet pipes A and B which can fill it in 12 minutes and 15 minutes respectively. An outlet pipe C can empty the full cistern in 10 minutes. If all three pipes are opened together, in what time will the cistern be filled?

#### Solution
1.  Assume Cistern Capacity = LCM of 12, 15, and 10 = 60 units.
2.  Rates: A = +5, B = +4, C = -6.
3.  Net Rate = $5 + 4 - 6 = 3$ units/minute.
4.  Time to fill = $60 / 3 = 20$ minutes.

### Q5
A and B can complete a work in 18 days and 24 days respectively. They work together for 6 days and then A leaves. Find the time B takes to complete the remaining work.

#### Solution
1.  Assume Total Work = LCM of 18 and 24 = 72 units.
2.  Efficiencies: A = 4, B = 3.
3.  Work done in 6 days $= 6 	imes (4 + 3) = 6 	imes 7 = 42$ units.
4.  Remaining work $= 72 - 42 = 30$ units.
5.  Time taken by B $= 30 / 3 = 10$ days.

### Q11
A, B, and C are employed to do a work for 5,290 Rupees. A and B together are supposed to do $19/23$ of the work, and B and C together $8/23$ of the work. Find A's share.

#### Solution

2.  $A + B = 19/23 \implies C = 1 - 19/23 = 4/23$.
3.  $B + C = 8/23 \implies A = 1 - 8/23 = 15/23$.

    $$	ext{A's share} = rac{15}{23} 	imes 5290 = 15 	imes 230 = 3450 	ext{ Rupees}$$

### Q12
An inlet pipe can fill a pool in 20 hours, and a drain can empty it in 30 hours. If both are opened, how long to fill the pool?

#### Solution
1.  LCM of 20 and 30 = 60.
2.  Inflow rate = +3, Outflow rate = -2.
3.  Net rate = +1 unit/hour.
4.  Time to fill = $60 / 1 = 60$ hours.

### Q13
If A does a work in 10 days and B in 20 days, and they work together for 4 days, what fraction of work is left?

#### Solution
1.  LCM of 10 and 20 = 20. Rates: A = 2, B = 1.
2.  Work done in 4 days $= 4 	imes 3 = 12$ units.
3.  Remaining work $= 20 - 12 = 8$ units.
4.  Fraction left $= 8 / 20 = 2/5$.
