## 2. The LCM Method

Instead of fractions, work with integer work units:
1.  **Total Work** = LCM of individual times.
2.  **Efficiency** = Total Work / Individual Time.
3.  **Combined Efficiency** = Sum of individual efficiencies.
4.  **Combined Time** = Total Work / Combined Efficiency.
