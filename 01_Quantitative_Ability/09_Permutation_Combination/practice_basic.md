# Permutation & Combination — Practice Basic (15 Questions)

**Target:** 100% | **Time:** 15 minutes

### Q1
Find 5! (5 factorial).
- **Answer:** 120 | 5×4×3×2×1 = 120

### Q2
In how many ways can 4 people be arranged in a line?
- **Answer:** 24 | 4! = 24

### Q3
How many 2-digit numbers can be formed from {1,2,3,4,5} without repetition?
- **Answer:** 20 | 5P2 = 5×4 = 20

### Q4
How many ways to choose 2 items from 6?
- **Answer:** 15 | 6C2 = 15

### Q5
7C3 = ?
- **Answer:** 35 | 7!/(3!4!) = 35

### Q6
nP1 for n=8?
- **Answer:** 8 | 8P1 = 8

### Q7
Total subsets of {a,b,c}?
- **Answer:** 8 | 2^3 = 8

### Q8
10 people shake hands. Total handshakes?
- **Answer:** 45 | 10C2 = 45

### Q9
Arrange 3 of 5 books on a shelf?
- **Answer:** 60 | 5P3 = 60

### Q10
In how many ways can 6 people sit at a round table?
- **Answer:** 120 | (6-1)! = 120

### Q11
How many 3-digit numbers without repetition from digits 1-9?
- **Answer:** 504 | 9P3 = 504

### Q12
nC0 for any n?
- **Answer:** 1 | Always 1

### Q13
5C5 = ?
- **Answer:** 1 | Only 1 way to select all

### Q14
10C9 = ?
- **Answer:** 10 | Same as 10C1 = 10

### Q15
How many triangles can be formed from 7 points (no 3 collinear)?
- **Answer:** 35 | 7C3 = 35
