# Permutation & Combination — Worked Examples

## Example 1 — Word Arrangements

**Problem:** In how many ways can the letters of MONDAY be arranged?

**Solution:**
- MONDAY has 6 distinct letters.
- Arrangements = 6! = 720

**Answer: 720 ways**

---

## Example 2 — Vowels Together

**Problem:** In how many ways can MONDAY be arranged with vowels always together?

**Solution:**
- Vowels in MONDAY: O, A (2 vowels)
- Treat OA as 1 unit: 5 units to arrange = 5! = 120
- Vowels can be arranged among themselves: 2! = 2
- Total = 120 × 2 = **240**

---

## Example 3 — Committee Selection

**Problem:** A committee of 3 men and 2 women is to be formed from 6 men and 5 women. How many ways?

**Solution:**
- Select 3 men from 6: 6C3 = 20
- Select 2 women from 5: 5C2 = 10
- Total = 20 × 10 = **200**

---

## Example 4 — Circular Seating

**Problem:** In how many ways can 5 people be seated around a circular table?

**Solution:**
- Circular arrangements = (5-1)! = 4! = **24**

---

## Example 5 — Word with Repeated Letters

**Problem:** How many distinct arrangements does BANANA have?

**Solution:**
- BANANA: B=1, A=3, N=2 → Total letters = 6
- Arrangements = 6! / (3! × 2! × 1!) = 720 / (6 × 2 × 1) = **60**
