# Permutation & Combination — Top 20 TCS Questions

### Q1 (High)
5P3 = ?
- **Answer:** 60 | 5×4×3

### Q2 (High)
6C2 = ?
- **Answer:** 15 | 6×5/2

### Q3 (High)
Arrangements of MATHEMATICS?
- **Answer:** 11!/(2!2!2!) = 4,989,600

### Q4 (High)
Committee of 3 from 10?
- **Answer:** 10C3 = 120

### Q5 (High)
5 people in circular arrangement?
- **Answer:** (5-1)! = 24

### Q6 (High)
How many 3-digit numbers from {1,2,3,4,5,6} without repetition?
- **Answer:** 6P3 = 120

### Q7 (High)
Words from PENCIL with vowels together?
- **Vowels:** E,I (2); treat as 1 unit → 5! × 2! = 240

### Q8 (Medium)
From 6 men, 4 women: teams of 4 with at least 1 woman?
- **Answer:** Total - all men = 10C4 - 6C4 = 210-15 = **195**

### Q9 (High)
10 people shake hands once. Total?
- **Answer:** 10C2 = 45

### Q10 (High)
Arrangements of BANANA?
- **Answer:** 6!/(3!2!) = 60

### Q11 (Medium)
4-letter words from PROBLEM?
- **Answer:** 7P4 = 840

### Q12 (High)
5 boys and 3 girls select team of 4, must have 2 girls minimum?
- **Answer:** 3C2×5C2 + 3C3×5C1 = 30+5 = 35

### Q13 (Medium)
How many even numbers of 4 digits from {1,2,3,4,5}?
- Even last digit: 2 or 4; 2 choices × 4P3 = 2×24 = **48** (wait: 4P3 = 24, so 2×24=48; but first position can't be 0 — no 0 in set, so all good)

### Q14 (Medium)
Triangles from 12 points (no 3 collinear)?
- **Answer:** 12C3 = 220

### Q15 (High)
6 friends invite to dinner: at least 1 must come?
- **Answer:** 2^6 - 1 = 63

### Q16 (Medium)
Arrangements of MISSISSIPPI?
- M=1,I=4,S=4,P=2: 11!/(4!4!2!) = **34,650**

### Q17 (Medium)
4-digit numbers using 1-9 divisible by 2?
- Even last digit options: 2,4,6,8 (4 choices)
- Remaining 3 positions from 8 remaining: 8P3 = 336
- Total = 4×336 = **1344**

### Q18 (Medium)
10 students: elect president and secretary (different)?
- 10P2 = 10×9 = **90**

### Q19 (High)
Total diagonals in hexagon?
- nC2 - n = 6C2-6 = 15-6 = **9**

### Q20 (Medium)
Password: 4 letters (A-Z) then 2 digits (0-9), no repetition?
- 26P4 × 10P2 = (26×25×24×23) × (10×9) = 358800 × 90 = **32,292,000**
