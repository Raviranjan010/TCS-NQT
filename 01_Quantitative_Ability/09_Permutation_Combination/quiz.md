# Permutation & Combination — Rapid Quiz

## Q&A Pairs

Q1: 4P2 = ?
A1: 12 (4×3)

Q2: 5C3 = ?
A2: 10 (5×4×3/3!)

Q3: Circular arrangements of 4?
A3: 3! = 6

Q4: Total subsets of 5 elements?
A4: 32 (2^5)

Q5: Handshakes between 8 people?
A5: 28 (8C2)

Q6: Arrangements of NOON?
A6: 4!/(2!2!) = 6

Q7: 4-digit numbers from 1-5 (no repeat), starting with 3?
A7: 1 × 4P3 = 24

Q8: Committee of 4 from 9?
A8: 9C4 = 126

Q9: Words from SMILE with S and E at ends?
A9: Fix S, E positions: 3! = 6 arrangements of middle letters; × 2 (S first or E first) = 12

Q10: 3 prizes among 10 (one each, different prizes)?
A10: 10P3 = 720

## True/False

1. nCr = nC(n-r) → **TRUE**
2. 5P3 > 5C3 always → **TRUE**
3. Circular seating of n = n! → **FALSE** (it's (n-1)!)
4. At least 1 from n items = 2^n - 1 → **TRUE**
5. For vowels together: count only vowel arrangements → **FALSE** (must also arrange the remaining units)
