# Permutation & Combination — Previous Year Questions

### PYQ1 (TCS 2023)
In how many ways can 3 boys and 2 girls be arranged in a line if all girls must be together?
- Girls bundle + 3 boys = 4 units: 4! × 2! = 24×2 = **48**

### PYQ2 (TCS 2023)
8C3 = ?
- 8×7×6/(3×2×1) = **56**

### PYQ3 (TCS 2022)
Words from LOGIC?
- 5! = **120**

### PYQ4 (TCS 2022)
Committee of 5 from 10: 3 specific people must always be included?
- 3 fixed, choose 2 more from remaining 7: 7C2 = **21**

### PYQ5 (TCS 2022)
How many arrangements of MANGO?
- 5! = **120**

### PYQ6 (TCS 2021)
4-digit numbers from digits 0-5, no repetition, first digit ≠ 0?
- First digit: 1-5 (5 choices), remaining 3 from 5 digits: 5P3 = 60
- Total = 5×60 = **300**

### PYQ7 (TCS 2021)
From 6 boys, 4 girls: pick 4 with at least 1 girl?
- Total - all boys = 10C4 - 6C4 = 210-15 = **195**

### PYQ8 (TCS 2021)
How many ways to seat 6 people at a round table?
- (6-1)! = **120**

### PYQ9 (TCS 2022)
4-letter passwords from 26 letters (no repetition)?
- 26P4 = 26×25×24×23 = **358,800**

### PYQ10 (TCS 2023)
How many triangles from 8 non-collinear points?
- 8C3 = **56**

### PYQ11 (TCS 2023)
Arrangements of LEVEL?
- L=2, E=2: 5!/(2!2!) = **30**

### PYQ12 (TCS 2022)
Total subsets of {1,2,3,4,5}?
- 2^5 = **32**

### PYQ13 (TCS 2021)
From 5 men, 4 women: how many groups of 3 men and 2 women?
- 5C3 × 4C2 = 10×6 = **60**

### PYQ14 (TCS 2023)
3 prizes among 10 students (different prizes, 1 per student)?
- 10P3 = **720**

### PYQ15 (TCS 2022)
All arrangements of AABB?
- 4!/(2!2!) = **6**
