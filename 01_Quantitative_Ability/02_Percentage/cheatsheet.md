## 2. Core Formulas

### Percentage Change
$$\text{Change \%} = \frac{\text{Final} - \text{Initial}}{\text{Initial}} \times 100$$

### Successive Percentage Change
$$\text{Net Change} = A + B + \frac{AB}{100}$$

### Product Constancy Rules
*   If Price increases by $\frac{1}{x}$, Consumption decreases by $\frac{1}{x+1}$ (Expenditure constant).
*   If Price decreases by $\frac{1}{x}$, Consumption increases by $\frac{1}{x-1}$ (Expenditure constant).

### Population Growth & Depreciation
$$P_{\text{final}} = P_{\text{initial}} \times \left(1 \pm \frac{R}{100}\right)^n$$
*(Use $+$ for growth, $-$ for depreciation)*
