# Percentage — Rapid Quiz (10 Q&As + 5 True/False)

Test your conceptual understanding and speed with this quick self-assessment.

## Part 1 — 10 Rapid Q&A Pairs

### Q1
In A's income is 50% more than B's, what is the ratio of A's income to B's?
*   **Answer:** $3:2$
*   **Explanation:** $A = 1.5B \implies A/B = 1.5 = 3/2$.

### Q2
What is the single equivalent discount of successive discounts of 20% and 20%?
*   **Answer:** $36\%$
*   **Explanation:** $20 + 20 - 4 = 36\%$.

### Q3
If a length of a rectangle increases by 20% and width decreases by 20%, what is the net change in area?
*   **Answer:** $4\%$ decrease
*   **Explanation:** $20 - 20 - 4 = -4\%$.

### Q4
Convert $37.5\%$ to its simplest fraction.
*   **Answer:** $\frac{3}{8}$
*   **Explanation:** $37.5\% = 3 \times 12.5\% = 3 \times \frac{1}{8} = \frac{3}{8}$.

### Q5
What is the multiplier for a $12.5\%$ increase?
*   **Answer:** $1.125$ (or $\frac{9}{8}$)
*   **Explanation:** $1 + 0.125 = 1.125$.

### Q6
If price of wheat decreases by 25%, by what fraction must consumption increase to keep expenditure same?
*   **Answer:** $\frac{1}{3}$ (or $33.33\%$)
*   **Explanation:** Decrease is $25\% = \frac{1}{4}$. Increase required is $\frac{1}{4-1} = \frac{1}{3}$.

### Q7
If $X$ increases to $1.25X$, what is the percentage increase?
*   **Answer:** $25\%$
*   **Explanation:** $(1.25 - 1) \times 100 = 25\%$.

### Q8
Calculate $10\%$ of $20\%$ of $30\%$ of 1000.
*   **Answer:** 6
*   **Explanation:** $1000 \times 0.3 \times 0.2 \times 0.1 = 6000 / 1000 = 6$.

### Q9
If $30\%$ of $A = 60$ Rupees, what is $150\%$ of $A$?
*   **Answer:** 300 Rupees
*   **Explanation:** $150\%$ is 5 times of $30\% \rightarrow 5 \times 60 = 300$.

### Q10
What is the percentage increase if value doubles?
*   **Answer:** $100\%$
*   **Explanation:** $\frac{2X - X}{X} \times 100 = 100\%$.

## Part 2 — 5 True/False Statements

### 1. Statement
A successive increase of $10\%$ followed by a decrease of $10\%$ always results in no change in the final value.
*   **Answer:** **FALSE**
*   **Explanation:** It results in a net decrease of $1\%$, as $10 - 10 - \frac{100}{100} = -1\%$.

### 2. Statement
The order of applying successive percentage changes does not affect the final value.
*   **Answer:** **TRUE**
*   **Explanation:** Multiplication is commutative ($X \times 1.1 \times 0.9 = X \times 0.9 \times 1.1$).

### 3. Statement
Percentage points represent the absolute difference between two percentage figures.
*   **Answer:** **TRUE**
*   **Explanation:** If a rate increases from $10\%$ to $15\%$, it is a 5 percentage point increase.

### 4. Statement
If A's salary is $20\%$ more than B's salary, then B's salary is $20\%$ less than A's.
*   **Answer:** **FALSE**
*   **Explanation:** B's salary is $16.67\%$ less than A's because the base value shifts to A's larger salary.

### 5. Statement
To find the original value before a $25\%$ increase, you divide the final value by $1.25$.
*   **Answer:** **TRUE**
*   **Explanation:** $\text{Final} = \text{Original} \times 1.25 \implies \text{Original} = \text{Final} / 1.25$.
