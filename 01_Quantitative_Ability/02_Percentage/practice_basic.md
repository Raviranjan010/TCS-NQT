# Percentage — Practice Basic (15 Questions)

This practice set contains 15 foundational questions testing direct conversions, basic growth, and base value identification.

*   **Suggested Time:** 15 minutes (60 seconds per question)
*   **Target Accuracy:** 100%

---

### Q1
Express $\frac{3}{8}$ as a percentage.
*   **Answer:** $37.5\%$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Percentage} = \text{Fraction} \times 100$$
    $$\frac{3}{8} \times 100 = \frac{300}{8} = 37.5\%$$

### Q2
What is $15\%$ of 80?
*   **Answer:** 12
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Value} = \frac{15}{100} \times 80 = 0.15 \times 80 = 12$$

### Q3
If $20\%$ of a number is 45, what is the number?
*   **Answer:** 225
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    Let the number be $X$.
    $$0.20 \times X = 45 \implies X = \frac{45}{0.20} = 225$$

### Q4
What percentage of 150 is 30?
*   **Answer:** $20\%$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Percentage} = \frac{30}{150} \times 100 = \frac{1}{5} \times 100 = 20\%$$

### Q5
Increase 120 by $25\%$.
*   **Answer:** 150
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{New Value} = 120 \times \left(1 + \frac{25}{100}\right) = 120 \times 1.25 = 150$$

### Q6
Decrease 80 by $10\%$.
*   **Answer:** 72
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{New Value} = 80 \times \left(1 - \frac{10}{100}\right) = 80 \times 0.90 = 72$$

### Q7
If a number decreases from 50 to 40, what is the percentage decrease?
*   **Answer:** $20\%$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Decrease \%} = \frac{50 - 40}{50} \times 100 = \frac{10}{50} \times 100 = 20\%$$

### Q8
If a number increases from 80 to 100, what is the percentage increase?
*   **Answer:** $25\%$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Increase \%} = \frac{100 - 80}{80} \times 100 = \frac{20}{80} \times 100 = 25\%$$

### Q9
What is the fractional equivalent of $66.67\%$?
*   **Answer:** $\frac{2}{3}$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    We know that $33.33\% = \frac{1}{3}$.
    $$66.67\% = 2 \times 33.33\% = 2 \times \frac{1}{3} = \frac{2}{3}$$

### Q10
What is the decimal equivalent of $7.5\%$?
*   **Answer:** $0.075$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Decimal} = \frac{7.5}{100} = 0.075$$

### Q11
If $x$ is $80\%$ of $y$, what percent of $x$ is $y$?
*   **Answer:** $125\%$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$x = 0.80y \implies y = \frac{x}{0.80} = 1.25x \implies 125\%$$

### Q12
Convert $\frac{1}{12}$ to a percentage.
*   **Answer:** $8.33\%$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Percentage} = \frac{1}{12} \times 100 = \frac{100}{12} = 8.33\%$$

### Q13
Calculate $50\%$ of $30\%$ of 200.
*   **Answer:** 30
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Value} = 200 \times \frac{30}{100} \times \frac{50}{100} = 200 \times 0.30 \times 0.50 = 30$$

### Q14
If a price increases by $100\%$, what is the multiplier?
*   **Answer:** $2$
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Multiplier} = 1 + \frac{100}{100} = 1 + 1 = 2$$

### Q15
What is $0.5\%$ of 1000?
*   **Answer:** 5
*   **Step-by-Step Calculation:**
    $$\text{Value} = 1000 \times \frac{0.5}{100} = 10 \times 0.5 = 5$$
