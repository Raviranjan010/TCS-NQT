# TSD — Rapid Quiz

## Q&A Pairs

Q1: Speed = ? if D=120km, T=2hr
A1: 60 km/h

Q2: Convert 54 km/h to m/s
A2: 15 m/s (54 × 5/18)

Q3: Two trains opposite dir, 60+90. Relative speed?
A3: 150 km/h

Q4: Train 100m + platform 200m at 20 m/s. Time?
A4: 15 seconds (300/20)

Q5: Downstream=12, Upstream=4. Still water speed?
A5: 8 km/h

Q6: Round trip 60/40 km/h. Average speed?
A6: 48 km/h (harmonic mean)

Q7: Two objects start from same point in opposite directions, speeds 5 and 3 m/s. Gap after 10 sec?
A7: 80m (8×10)

Q8: A chases B. A=15m/s, B=10m/s, gap=100m. Time to catch?
A8: 20 sec (100/5)

Q9: Boat goes 30km downstream in 3h. Downstream speed?
A9: 10 km/h

Q10: Two trains 200m each cross in 20 sec (same dir, rel speed 20 m/s). Verify?
A10: Time = 400/20 = 20 sec ✓

## True/False

1. Upstream speed = Boat + Stream → **FALSE** (it's Boat - Stream)
2. For equal distances, average speed = harmonic mean → **TRUE**
3. Two trains opposite direction: relative speed = sum of speeds → **TRUE**
4. A train crossing a pole needs both train and pole length → **FALSE** (only train length)
5. km/h × 18/5 converts to m/s → **FALSE** (that converts m/s → km/h; km/h × 5/18 → m/s)
