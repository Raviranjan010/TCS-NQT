# Time, Speed & Distance — Revision Notes

## Core Formula
$$S = D/T \quad D = S \times T \quad T = D/S$$

## Unit Conversion
- km/h → m/s: × 5/18
- m/s → km/h: × 18/5

## Relative Speed
| Direction | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| Opposite | S1 + S2 |
| Same | \|S1 - S2\| |

## Trains
- Pole: L/S
- Platform: (L+P)/S
- Two trains: (L1+L2)/(S1±S2)

## Boats
- Downstream = B + S
- Upstream = B - S
- Still water = (D+U)/2
- Stream = (D-U)/2

## Average Speed
- Equal distance: 2S1S2/(S1+S2)
- Equal time: (S1+S2)/2
