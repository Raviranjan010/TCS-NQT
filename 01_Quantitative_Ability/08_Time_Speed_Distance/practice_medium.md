# Time, Speed & Distance — Practice Medium (15 Questions)

**Target:** 85%+ accuracy | **Time:** 30 minutes

### Q1
A train 150m long crosses a platform 250m long in 40 seconds. Find speed in km/h.
#### Solution
- Total distance = 150 + 250 = 400 m
- Speed = 400/40 = 10 m/s = 10 × 18/5 = **36 km/h**

### Q2
Two trains of lengths 180m and 220m approach each other at 60 km/h and 40 km/h. Time to cross?
#### Solution
- Relative speed = 100 km/h = 100 × 5/18 = 27.78 m/s
- Total length = 400m
- Time = 400/27.78 ≈ **14.4 seconds**

### Q3
A boat covers 32 km downstream in 4 hours and 16 km upstream in 4 hours. Find boat speed in still water.
#### Solution
- Downstream speed = 32/4 = 8 km/h
- Upstream speed = 16/4 = 4 km/h
- Still water speed = (8+4)/2 = **6 km/h**

### Q4
Ram drives from city A to B at 50 km/h and returns at 70 km/h. Find average speed.
#### Solution
- Average speed = 2(50)(70)/(50+70) = 7000/120 = **58.33 km/h**

### Q5
A thief at 30 km/h is chased by a cop at 40 km/h. If cop starts 10 min late, when does he catch the thief?
#### Solution
- In 10 min thief travels: 30 × (10/60) = 5 km head start
- Relative speed = 40 - 30 = 10 km/h
- Catch time = 5/10 = 0.5 hours = **30 minutes** after cop starts

### Q6
A train crosses a man standing on platform in 15 seconds and the 200m platform in 25 seconds. Find train length.
#### Solution
- Let train length = L
- L/speed = 15 ... (i)
- (L+200)/speed = 25 ... (ii)
- Dividing (ii)/(i): (L+200)/L = 25/15 → 15L+3000 = 25L → 10L=3000 → **L = 300m**

### Q7
A and B start walking toward each other from towns 100 km apart. A at 5 km/h, B at 3 km/h. When and where do they meet?
#### Solution
- Combined speed = 8 km/h
- Time to meet = 100/8 = 12.5 hours
- A travels 5 × 12.5 = 62.5 km from A's town
- **62.5 km from A's town, 12.5 hours**

### Q8
A boat takes twice as long upstream as downstream. Stream speed is 4 km/h. Find still water speed.
#### Solution
- Let still water speed = B
- Upstream speed = B-4, Downstream speed = B+4
- Time ratio upstream:downstream = 2:1, so speed ratio downstream:upstream = 2:1
- (B+4)/(B-4) = 2 → B+4 = 2B-8 → **B = 12 km/h**

### Q9
Two trains start from stations A and B simultaneously toward each other. Speeds are 70 and 50 km/h. Distance = 240 km. Find meeting point.
#### Solution
- Time to meet = 240/120 = 2 hours
- Train from A travels 70×2 = 140 km from A
- **Meeting point is 140 km from A**

### Q10
A man walks 3/4 his usual speed and reaches office 20 min late. Find usual time.
#### Solution
- Usual speed S, new speed = 3S/4
- At 3/4 speed, time = 4/3 of usual time
- Extra time = 1/3 of usual time = 20 min
- Usual time = **60 minutes**

### Q11
A train 300m long moving at 45 km/h overtakes another 200m long moving at 30 km/h in same direction. Time?
#### Solution
- Relative speed = 15 km/h = 15×5/18 = 4.167 m/s
- Total length = 500m
- Time = 500/4.167 = **120 seconds**

### Q12
A man can row upstream at 6 km/h and downstream at 10 km/h. Find time to cover 45 km in still water.
#### Solution
- Still water speed = (6+10)/2 = 8 km/h
- Time = 45/8 = **5.625 hours**

### Q13
Train A leaves at 9am at 60 km/h. Train B leaves at 10am from same station at 90 km/h. At what time does B overtake A?
#### Solution
- A's head start = 60 km
- Relative speed = 30 km/h
- Time to overtake = 60/30 = 2 hours after 10am = **12 noon**

### Q14
In 12 hours a person goes 30 km by boat and 40 km by cycle at 40 km/h. Find boat speed.
#### Solution
- Cycle time = 40/40 = 1 hour
- Boat time = 12 - 1 = 11 hours
- Boat speed = 30/11 ≈ **2.73 km/h** (check numbers: typical exam would give clean answers)

### Q15
A car increases speed from 40 to 80 km/h. The journey that took 4 hours now takes how long?
#### Solution
- Distance = 40 × 4 = 160 km
- New time = 160/80 = **2 hours**
