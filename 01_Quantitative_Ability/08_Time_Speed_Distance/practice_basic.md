# Time, Speed & Distance — Practice Basic (15 Questions)

**Target:** 100% accuracy | **Time:** 15 minutes

### Q1
A car travels 180 km in 3 hours. Find speed in km/h.
- **Answer:** 60 km/h | Speed = 180/3 = 60 km/h

### Q2
A train moving at 72 km/h. Convert to m/s.
- **Answer:** 20 m/s | 72 × 5/18 = 20

### Q3
A person walks 10 km in 2.5 hours. Find speed.
- **Answer:** 4 km/h | Speed = 10/2.5 = 4

### Q4
A car at 60 km/h covers a distance in 2.5 hours. Find distance.
- **Answer:** 150 km | D = 60 × 2.5 = 150

### Q5
Two cars travel toward each other at 40 and 60 km/h from 200 km apart. Time to meet?
- **Answer:** 2 hours | Time = 200/(40+60) = 2

### Q6
A train 100m long crosses a pole in 5 seconds. Find speed in m/s.
- **Answer:** 20 m/s | Speed = 100/5 = 20

### Q7
Downstream speed = 18 km/h, upstream = 6 km/h. Find still water speed.
- **Answer:** 12 km/h | (18+6)/2 = 12

### Q8
Find stream speed if downstream = 18, upstream = 6.
- **Answer:** 6 km/h | (18-6)/2 = 6

### Q9
A person walks at 5 km/h. Time to cover 20 km?
- **Answer:** 4 hours | T = 20/5 = 4

### Q10
Two trains in same direction: speeds 80 and 60 km/h. Relative speed?
- **Answer:** 20 km/h | 80 - 60 = 20

### Q11
Two trains in opposite directions: 60 and 90 km/h. Relative speed?
- **Answer:** 150 km/h | 60 + 90 = 150

### Q12
Convert 30 m/s to km/h.
- **Answer:** 108 km/h | 30 × 18/5 = 108

### Q13
A train 200m long crosses a 300m platform at 20 m/s. Time?
- **Answer:** 25 seconds | (200+300)/20 = 25

### Q14
Car P at 80 km/h leaves at 6am. Car Q at 100 km/h leaves at 7am. At same direction. When does Q catch P?
- **Answer:** 11am | Head start = 80 km; Catch time = 80/(100-80) = 4 hours after 7am = 11am

### Q15
A man rows 10 km downstream in 1 hour and 5 km upstream in 1 hour. Still water speed?
- **Answer:** 7.5 km/h | D=10, U=5; Still = (10+5)/2 = 7.5
