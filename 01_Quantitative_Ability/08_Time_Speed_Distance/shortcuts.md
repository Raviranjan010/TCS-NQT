# Time, Speed & Distance — Shortcuts

## 1. The 5/18 Conversion Shortcut
For km/h → m/s: multiply by 5/18 (or divide by 3.6)
For m/s → km/h: multiply by 18/5 (or multiply by 3.6)

Quick table:
| km/h | m/s |
|------|-----|
| 18   | 5   |
| 36   | 10  |
| 54   | 15  |
| 72   | 20  |
| 90   | 25  |
| 108  | 30  |

## 2. Train Crossing Shortcut
For two trains crossing:
- **Always add lengths** in the numerator (regardless of direction)
- **Opposite direction:** Add speeds → faster solution
- **Same direction:** Subtract speeds → careful about which is faster

## 3. Average Speed Quick Check
If a round trip at speeds S1 and S2:
- Avg speed is ALWAYS less than (S1 + S2)/2
- Avg speed = 2S1S2/(S1+S2) — only when distances are equal

## 4. Boats: Sum and Difference Rule
If given downstream speed (D) and upstream speed (U):
- Still water speed = (D + U) / 2
- Stream speed = (D - U) / 2
No algebra needed!

## 5. Meeting Point Shortcut
Two people A and B walk toward each other from points X and Y:
- They meet when combined distance = XY
- Meeting point splits XY in ratio of speeds: A:B = Sa:Sb

## 6. Proportional Thinking
"Speed is doubled, time is halved" — use ratio to find new time/speed without full algebra.
