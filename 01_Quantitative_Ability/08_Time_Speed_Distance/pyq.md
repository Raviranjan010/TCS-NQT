# Time, Speed & Distance — Previous Year Questions

### PYQ1
A train passes a platform 100m long in 60 sec and a pole in 40 sec. Find train speed.
- L/40 = (L+100)/60 → 60L = 40L+4000 → 20L=4000 → L=200m
- Speed = 200/40 = **5 m/s = 18 km/h**

### PYQ2
Boat downstream: 30 km in 2h. Upstream: 30 km in 6h. Stream speed?
- Downstream=15, Upstream=5; Stream = (15-5)/2 = **5 km/h**

### PYQ3
Car A at 60 km/h leaves at 7am. Car B at 90 km/h leaves at 9am in same direction. When does B overtake?
- A's head start at 9am = 120 km; Relative = 30; Time = 4h → **1pm**

### PYQ4
Two trains 200m each in opposite directions at 45 and 55 km/h. Crossing time?
- Relative = 100 km/h = 250/9 m/s; Total = 400m; Time = 400÷(250/9) = **14.4 sec**

### PYQ5
A man rows 12 km downstream in 2h and 8 km upstream in 2h. Find still water speed.
- D=6, U=4; Speed = **5 km/h**

### PYQ6
If speed is reduced by 20%, by what % does time increase (for same distance)?
- New speed = 80% → Time = 1/0.8 = 1.25 of original → **25% increase**

### PYQ7
A person travels from A to B at 50 km/h and returns at 30 km/h. Average speed?
- Avg = 2×50×30/80 = 3000/80 = **37.5 km/h**

### PYQ8
Thief at 8 m/s is chased by cop at 10 m/s. Thief has 100m head start. Time to catch?
- Time = 100/(10-8) = **50 seconds**

### PYQ9
Train 500m long at 90 km/h. Time to cross a bridge of 1500m?
- Total = 2000m; Speed = 25 m/s; Time = **80 seconds**

### PYQ10
A walks 6 km/h and B walks 4 km/h. They start from same point toward same destination 30 km away. How far ahead is A when B reaches halfway?
- B reaches 15 km; Time for B = 15/4 = 3.75 hrs; A covers 6×3.75 = 22.5 km
- A is **22.5 - 15 = 7.5 km ahead of B** at that point

### PYQ11
Find avg speed: first half time at 40 km/h, second half time at 60 km/h.
- Avg = (40+60)/2 = **50 km/h** (equal TIME, so arithmetic mean applies!)

### PYQ12
Two trains each 120m long cross each other in 12 sec. Combined speed in m/s?
- (120+120)/12 = 240/12 = **20 m/s**

### PYQ13
A boat takes 3 hours to go 24 km downstream and 6 hours to come back. Find still water speed.
- D=8, U=4; Speed = **6 km/h**

### PYQ14
A car travels first 30 km at 60 km/h and next 30 km at 30 km/h. Average speed?
- Time1 = 0.5h, Time2 = 1h; Avg = 60/1.5 = **40 km/h**

### PYQ15
A man walks to office at 5 km/h and returns by bike at 25 km/h. Average speed?
- Avg = 2×5×25/30 = 250/30 = **8.33 km/h**
