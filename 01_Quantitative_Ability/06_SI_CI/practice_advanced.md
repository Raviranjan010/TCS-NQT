### Q7
A sum of money is lent out at simple interest. It amounts to 720 Rupees in 2 years and to 1,020 Rupees in 7 years. Find the principal sum.

#### Standard Method
1.  Amount in 2 years $= P + 2I = 720$.
2.  Amount in 7 years $= P + 7I = 1020$.
3.  Subtract the first from the second:
    $$5I = 300 \implies I = 60 	ext{ Rupees per year}$$
4.  Principal $= 720 - 2(60) = 720 - 120 = 600$ Rupees.

#### Alternate Fast Method
*   Interest for 5 years $= 1020 - 720 = 300 \implies 60$ Rupees/year.
*   Principal $= 720 - 2 	imes 60 = 600$ Rupees.
