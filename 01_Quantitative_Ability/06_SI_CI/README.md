# Simple & Compound Interest - Module Overview

This module covers the mathematical principles of Simple Interest (SI), Compound Interest (CI), annual compounding, multiple-year SI-CI difference relations, and loan installments.

---

## Metadata

*   **TCS NQT Frequency:** `🔴 High` (Appears as 2 direct word problems, often involving SI-CI difference or installments)
*   **Difficulty Level:** `🟡 Medium` to `🔴 Hard`
*   **Core Pre-requisites:** Percentages, Successive percentage changes, Equations

---

## Study Guide & Syllabus

1.  **Simple Interest:** Linear growth, formulas, and multiple-year sums.
2.  **Compound Interest:** Exponential growth, compounding intervals (yearly, half-yearly, quarterly).
3.  **SI-CI Differences:** Specific shortcuts for 2-year and 3-year difference calculations.
4.  **Installment Schemes:** Calculating equal yearly installments for both Simple Interest and Compound Interest loans.
