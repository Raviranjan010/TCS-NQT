### SET A — Table: Department Revenue (Crores)

| Department | 2022 | 2023 |
|------------|------|------|
| Sales      | 120  | 150  |
| Marketing  | 80   | 90   |
| IT         | 200  | 240  |
| HR         | 40   | 35   |
| Finance    | 160  | 180  |
| **Total**  | 600  | 695  |

### Q1 (High Frequency)
Which department had the highest % increase from 2022 to 2023?
- Sales: 30/120 = 25%
- Marketing: 10/80 = 12.5%
- IT: 40/200 = 20%
- Finance: 20/160 = 12.5%
- **Answer: Sales (25%)**

### Q2 (High Frequency)
What was the total revenue in 2022?
- 120+80+200+40+160 = **600 crores**

### Q3 (High Frequency)
Which department had a decrease?
- **HR: 40 → 35 (decrease of 5 crores)**

### Q4 (High Frequency)
Ratio of IT revenue (2022) to Finance revenue (2022)?
- 200:160 = **5:4**

### Q5 (High Frequency)
Average revenue across all departments in 2023?
- 695/5 = **139 crores**
