# 07 Syllogism - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

---

**Q1.** Statements: All laptops are computers. Some computers are calculators. Conclusions: I. Some laptops are calculators. II. No laptop is a calculator.
*   **Type:** Two Statements Syllogism (Either-Or Case)
*   **Answer:** Calculators overlaps with computers, but laptops may or may not overlap with calculators. Conclusions form a complementary pair. Answer: Either Conclusion I or II follows.
*   **Time target:** 35 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 85% of papers)
