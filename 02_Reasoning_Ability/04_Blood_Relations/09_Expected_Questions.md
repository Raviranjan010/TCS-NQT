# 04 Blood Relations - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

---

**Q1.** Pointing to a photograph, a man said, 'I have no brother or sister but that man's father is my father's son.' Who is in the photograph?
*   **Type:** Pointing to a picture representation
*   **Answer:** 'My father's son' with no siblings means the speaker himself. Thus, 'that man's father is the speaker himself'. The photo is of the speaker's son.
*   **Time target:** 40 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 70% of papers)
