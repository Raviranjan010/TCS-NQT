# 09 Floor Puzzles - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

---

**Q1.** Seven people live on a 7-floor building. A lives on an odd-numbered floor. Two people live between A and B. C lives immediately above B. Find C's floor number.
*   **Type:** Floor Puzzle with Constraints
*   **Answer:** Test odd floors for A. If A=3, B can be on floor 6 (two floors between). C is on floor 7. Satisfies all constraints.
*   **Time target:** 75 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** Medium (appears in 40% of papers)
