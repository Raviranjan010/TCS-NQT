This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

**Q1.** If 15th August 2024 was a Thursday, what day of the week will 15th August 2026 be?
*   **Type:** Calendar - Finding day with year skips
*   **Answer:** 2024 to 2025 = +1 day (ordinary year). 2025 to 2026 = +1 day (ordinary year). Total odd days = 2. Thursday + 2 days = Saturday.
*   **Time target:** 40 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** Medium (appears in 50% of papers)
