# 05 Directions - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

---

**Q1.** A person walks 6 km North, turns right and walks 13 km East. How far and in which direction is he now with respect to his starting point?
*   **Type:** Pythagoras Theorem & Direction Sense
*   **Answer:** Distance = sqrt(6^2 + 13^2) = 14 km. Direction is North-East.
*   **Time target:** 35 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 75% of papers)
