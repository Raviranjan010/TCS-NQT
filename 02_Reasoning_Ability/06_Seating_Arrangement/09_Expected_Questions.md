# 06 Seating Arrangement - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

**Q1.** Six people (A, B, C, D, E, F) are sitting in a circle facing the center. A is second to the left of B. C is immediate right of A. D is opposite to E. Who is sitting to the immediate left of F?
*   **Type:** Circular Seating Arrangement
*   **Answer:** Draw the circle and place B at position 1. A is at position 5. C is at position 6. E/D occupy remaining opposite slots. F sits at position 4. Immediate left of F is B.
*   **Time target:** 60 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 80% of papers)
