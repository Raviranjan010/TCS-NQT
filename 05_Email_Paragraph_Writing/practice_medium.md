# Email & Paragraph Writing — Practice: Medium

This practice set contains 3 email prompts and 2 paragraph writing prompts, each followed by a fully solved model response of medium difficulty.

---

## Part 1 — Email Writing Prompts

Write a professional email for each of the following scenarios. Each response must be between 80–120 words (body only).

### Prompt 1 — Budget Approval Request
*   **Scenario:** You need approval for a $500 budget to purchase an online research database subscription. Email your department head.
*   **Guidelines:** State the purpose, explain how it improves work efficiency, and detail the cost.

#### Model Solution
**Subject:** Budget Approval Request: Research Database Subscription ($500)

Dear Dr. Jenkins,

I am writing to formally request approval for a $500 budget to purchase a one-year subscription to the TechResearch online database. 

Currently, our market analysis team spends several hours locating verified industry data. Access to this comprehensive database would allow us to generate competitive intelligence reports twice as fast, significantly improving our overall work efficiency. The subscription cost is a flat annual fee of $500, which fits within our quarterly department allowance. 

I look forward to your approval.

Sincerely,

Raj Sumit  
Research Lead  
(Word Count: 88 words, excluding headers and signature)

### Prompt 2 — Requesting Late Project Inputs
*   **Scenario:** A client has not sent the design assets needed for coding. Email them.
*   **Guidelines:** Explain the project timeline risk, state exactly what assets are missing, and request them by a specific date.

#### Model Solution
**Subject:** Urgent: Outstanding Design Assets for Phase 3 Coding

Dear Mr. Vance,

I am writing to request the outstanding design assets required for the Phase 3 coding stage of your web portal. 

Specifically, we have not yet received the high-resolution vector logos and the typography style guide. As a result, our development timeline is at risk, and we may experience a delay in delivery. Could you please share these missing files by Friday, June 30, to help us keep the project on track? 

Thank you for your prompt assistance in resolving this matter.

Sincerely,

Raj Sumit  
Project Manager  
(Word Count: 87 words, excluding headers and signature)

### Prompt 3 — Project Status Weekly Update
*   **Scenario:** Email your manager updating them on the weekly milestones completed by your team.
*   **Guidelines:** Use bullet points for accomplishments and mention next week's focus.

#### Model Solution
**Subject:** Weekly Status Update: Project Apollo (Week Ending June 28)

Dear Ms. Albright,

I am writing to share our team's weekly status update for Project Apollo. 

We have successfully completed the following milestones:
*   Finished the backend database migration to AWS.
*   Resolved all critical login bugs identified during beta testing.
*   Conducted a security audit on the user profile module.

Next week, we will focus on frontend dashboard integration and preparing the final user documentation. Please let me know if you require further details.

Best regards,

Raj Sumit  
Team Lead  
(Word Count: 85 words, excluding headers and signature)

## Part 2 — Paragraph Writing Prompts

Write a unified paragraph for each of the following topics. Each response must be between 120–150 words.

### Prompt 4 — The Impact of Single-Use Plastics
*   **Structure:** Intro $\rightarrow$ Ecological damage $\rightarrow$ Example (ocean life stats) $\rightarrow$ Conclusion.
*   **Guidelines:** Focus on environmental longevity of plastics.

#### Model Solution
The widespread use of single-use plastics has precipitated a catastrophic ecological crisis in our marine and terrestrial environments. Because these synthetic materials are non-biodegradable, they persist in ecosystems for hundreds of years, breaking down only into toxic microplastics that contaminate soil and water systems. For example, recent environmental reports estimate that over eight million metric tons of plastic enter our oceans annually, leading to the ingestion of plastic debris by ninety percent of seabirds and marine mammals. This persistent pollution threatens global biodiversity and bioaccumulates up the food chain, eventually impacting human health. In conclusion, mitigating the damage caused by single-use plastics requires strict regulatory bans and an immediate global transition toward compostable and sustainable packaging alternatives before the ecological degradation becomes completely irreversible. (132 words)

### Prompt 5 — Challenges of Online Education
*   **Structure:** Intro $\rightarrow$ Lack of physical interaction/attention issues $\rightarrow$ Example (distraction during live classes) $\rightarrow$ Conclusion.
*   **Guidelines:** Balance the convenience of online learning with its psychological barriers.

#### Model Solution
While online education offers unprecedented convenience, it also presents significant psychological and social challenges due to the lack of physical classroom interaction. Without the structured environment of a physical school, many students struggle with self-regulation, leading to severe attention deficits and academic disengagement. Additionally, the lack of face-to-face peer communication can foster feelings of isolation and impede the development of essential social skills. For instance, recent educational surveys indicate that over sixty percent of students report feeling distracted during live virtual sessions, citing domestic disturbances and digital fatigue as primary barriers. This highlights that passive screen time cannot replicate the active learning dynamics of a physical classroom. In conclusion, addressing the shortcomings of digital learning requires a transition to hybrid models that balance virtual convenience with necessary in-person social engagement. (133 words)
