# Email & Paragraph Writing

Email and Paragraph Writing are key components of the TCS NQT Business Communication section, testing your ability to construct professional, coherent, and grammatically correct written communication.

- **TCS NQT Weightage**: 🟢 Medium to High — typically 1 email prompt and 1 paragraph writing prompt in the Business Communication section.
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Expected Questions**: Writing a formal email based on a scenario, or drafting a short paragraph on a socio-economic/technology topic using transitions.
