# Email & Paragraph Writing — Practice: Basic

This practice set contains 3 email prompts and 2 paragraph writing prompts, each followed by a fully solved model response adhering to the structures taught in the notes.

---

## Part 1 — Email Writing Prompts

Write a professional email for each of the following scenarios. Each response must be between 80–120 words (body only).

### Prompt 1 — Casual Sick Leave
*   **Scenario:** You need to take a day off due to a severe headache. Email your supervisor.
*   **Guidelines:** Mention the date of absence, assign backup contact for urgent items, and keep the tone professional.

#### Model Solution
**Subject:** Leave Request: Sick Leave - June 28 (Emp ID: 4492)

Dear Mr. Gupta,

I am writing to request a day of sick leave for today, June 28, due to a sudden and severe headache. Consequently, I will be unable to log in and fulfill my daily project responsibilities. 

To ensure project continuity, I have requested Rohit to monitor my critical tasks and address any urgent queries during my absence. I expect to recover and return to work tomorrow morning. 

Thank you for your time and understanding.

Sincerely,

Raj Sumit  
Software Developer  
(Word Count: 85 words, excluding headers and signature)

### Prompt 2 — Meeting Reschedule Request
*   **Scenario:** You have a conflicting client call and need to postpone an internal team meeting.
*   **Guidelines:** Propose a new date/time and state the reason clearly.

#### Model Solution
**Subject:** Reschedule Request: Weekly Team Sync (June 28)

Dear Ms. Mehta,

I am writing to request a reschedule for our internal team sync meeting scheduled for today at 3:00 PM. Unfortunately, a last-minute conflict has arisen due to an urgent client call that cannot be postponed. 

Therefore, I propose that we reschedule our team meeting to tomorrow, June 29, at 11:00 AM, as my schedule is entirely clear then. Could you please confirm if this new time slot works for you? 

Thank you for your flexibility and understanding.

Sincerely,

Raj Sumit  
Business Analyst  
(Word Count: 86 words, excluding headers and signature)

---

### Prompt 3 — Delivery Update Query
*   **Scenario:** You ordered office stationery that was due yesterday. Email the vendor for an update.
*   **Guidelines:** Reference the order number, mention the delay, and ask for a revised delivery date.

#### Model Solution
**Subject:** Delivery Status Query: Order #ST-99821

Dear Customer Service Team,

I am writing to inquire about the delivery status of the office stationery ordered under reference #ST-99821. The shipment was originally due yesterday, June 27, but has not yet arrived at our office. 

These supplies are essential for our upcoming corporate workshop scheduled on Monday, making any further delay highly disruptive to our schedule. Could you please provide a revised delivery date and a tracking link? 

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter.

Sincerely,

Raj Sumit  
Office Coordinator  
(Word Count: 87 words, excluding headers and signature)

## Part 2 — Paragraph Writing Prompts

Write a unified paragraph for each of the following topics. Each response must be between 120–150 words.

### Prompt 4 — The Importance of Morning Walks
*   **Structure:** Introduction $\rightarrow$ Explanation of health benefits $\rightarrow$ Example (daily routine impact) $\rightarrow$ Conclusion.
*   **Guidelines:** Focus on physical and mental health.

#### Model Solution
Engaging in a regular morning walk is an exceptionally beneficial practice that promotes both physical health and mental clarity. Walking at a brisk pace during the early hours increases cardiovascular circulation, strengthens muscle tissue, and enhances joint flexibility. Furthermore, exposure to early morning sunlight aids in the synthesis of essential vitamin D while stimulating the release of endorphins, which effectively reduce stress levels and improve cognitive function. For instance, recent public health studies indicate that individuals who participate in a thirty-minute walk every morning experience a forty percent reduction in anxiety and significantly higher energy levels throughout the workday compared to sedentary individuals. This simple activity requires no expensive equipment, making it an accessible preventive healthcare habit. In conclusion, incorporating a brief morning walk into one's daily routine establishes a solid foundation for a healthier, more balanced, and highly productive life. (142 words)

### Prompt 5 — Benefits of Reading Books
*   **Structure:** Introduction $\rightarrow$ Vocabulary/cognitive gains $\rightarrow$ Example (reading habit comparison) $\rightarrow$ Conclusion.
*   **Guidelines:** Contrast book reading with quick internet browsing.

#### Model Solution
Reading books offers profound cognitive and vocabulary benefits that far exceed the superficial gains of browsing digital content. Unlike rapid internet surfing, reading a book demands sustained attention, which strengthens the neural pathways associated with focus and deep comprehension. Additionally, literature exposes readers to complex grammatical structures and diverse vocabulary in context, which enhances overall verbal ability. For example, educational research shows that students who read books for twenty minutes daily score fifty percent higher on standardized vocabulary assessments than those who only consume social media content. This habit fosters critical thinking by forcing the mind to analyze plots and characters deeply. In conclusion, while internet browsing provides quick facts, reading books remains the ultimate tool for intellectual cultivation and long-term academic excellence. (126 words)
