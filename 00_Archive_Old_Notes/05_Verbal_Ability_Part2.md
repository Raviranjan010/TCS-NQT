## 1. Active Summary Comparison

To avoid plagiarism penalties, study how to transform key details into your own words without losing metrics.

| Original Sentence | Poor Rewrite (Plagiarized) | Excellent Rewrite (Clear) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| *RSA encryption, which secures ninety percent of internet transactions, could become obsolete.* | *RSA encryption, which secures ninety percent of internet transactions, will be obsolete.* | *A security standard protecting ninety percent of online transactions faces obsolescence.* |

### Practice Passage Recall Exercises

**Practice 1**
* **Original**: "Quantum computing threatens modern cryptography because of its ability to solve integer factorization problems exponentially faster than classical computers. RSA encryption, which secures ninety percent of internet transactions, could become obsolete within the next decade."
* **Hint**: Identify core details: Quantum computers break RSA; RSA protects 90% transactions; timeframe is 10 years.
* **Solution**: 
  * *Model Rewrite*: Quantum machines pose a severe threat to digital security by factoring large integers rapidly. Consequently, the RSA algorithm, which currently guards ninety percent of online payments, faces redundancy over the next ten years.

---

## 2. Outline Constraints Checklist

When writing the 9-minute email:
- `[ ]` Read the prompt and list all provided outline keywords in their exact sequence.
- `[ ]` Write complete sentences only (no bullet points or fragments).
- `[ ]` Verify that the word count is between 100 and 140 words.
- `[ ]` Use formal greetings (Dear Mr./Ms. [Name]) and standard professional sign-offs.

---

### Guided Practice Email Exercise

**Situation**: Write an email to your project manager requesting leave for a family emergency. Use the outline words in sequence: *emergency*, *urgent*, *travel*, *delegate*, *resume*.
* **Hint**: Write the outline words down. Ensure each word is used in a separate sentence in the given order.
* **Solution**: 
  * *Subject*: Leave Request Due to Family Emergency
  * *Email*:
    Dear Mr. Patel,
    I am writing to request immediate leave due to a sudden family **emergency** at home. There are several **urgent** domestic matters that require my personal attention. Therefore, I must **travel** to my hometown this evening. I will **delegate** my active sprint items to my team partner, Rohan. I expect to **resume** my duties next Monday.
    Best regards,
    Ravi Ranjan
