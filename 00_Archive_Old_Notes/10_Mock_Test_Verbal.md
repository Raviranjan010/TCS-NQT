## 📝 Section 1: Sentence Completion (Q1 – Q20)

### Q1. (MCQ)
The manager was so _______ by the team's poor performance that he canceled the weekend holiday.
* (A) elated
* (B) perturbed
* (C) indifferent
* (D) placated
* **Hint**: The cancellation of the holiday indicates the manager was upset or troubled.
* **Solution**:
  * *Pattern ID*: Verb-Mock-01
  * *Approach*: Analyze the context of the sentence. "Poor performance" and "canceled holiday" are negative indicators. The blank must represent a negative emotion.
  * *Option check*: Elated (positive), Indifferent (neutral), Placated (pacified/positive), Perturbed (disturbed/negative). Thus, (B) is correct.
  * *Rule*: Context Clue Alignment.

### Q2. (MCQ)
She decided to abstain _______ eating sweets to control her blood sugar levels.
* (A) from
* (B) for
* (C) in
* (D) with
* **Hint**: "Abstain" is always followed by a specific preposition denoting separation.
* **Solution**:
  * *Pattern ID*: Verb-Mock-02
  * *Approach*: Standard preposition pairing rule. "Abstain" pairs with "from".
  * *Correct answer*: (A) from.
  * *Rule*: Phrasal Verb collocation.

### Q3. (MCQ)
Identify the grammatically correct sentence:
* (A) Each of the students have completed their assignment on time.
* (B) Each of the students has completed his or her assignment on time.
* (C) Each of the student has completed their assignment on time.
* (D) Each of the students have completed his assignment on time.
* **Hint**: "Each of the" is followed by a plural noun, but takes a singular verb and singular pronouns.
* **Solution**:
  * *Pattern ID*: Verb-Mock-03
  * *Approach*: "Each of the students" has the plural noun "students" but the subject is "Each", which is singular. Thus, it needs "has" (singular verb) and "his or her" (singular pronoun).
  * *Correct answer*: (B).
  * *Rule*: Pronoun-Antecedent Agreement.
  * *Trap*: Using "have" because of proximity to the word "students".
