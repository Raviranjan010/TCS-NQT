# Verbal Ability PYQs - Flashcards

Interactive flashcards for self-testing.

---

## 📇 Flashcard Deck

### Card 1: Conditional Sentences
*   **Front**: If he ____ (study) harder, he would have passed the exam. Fill in the blank.
*   **Back**: **had studied**. (Type 3 conditional: "would have passed" requires past perfect "had studied").

### Card 2: Subject-Verb Agreement
*   **Front**: "Either the manager or the assistants ____ (be) responsible." Was or Were?
*   **Back**: **Were**. (Subject closest to verb is plural "assistants").

### Card 3: Prepositions
*   **Front**: "He is capable ____ doing this." What preposition follows "capable"?
*   **Back**: **of**. (Collocation: capable of).

### Card 4: Voice Conversion
*   **Front**: Passive voice of "Someone has stolen my wallet"?
*   **Back**: "My wallet **has been stolen**." (Agent "by someone" is omitted since it is indefinite).

### Card 5: Verb forms
*   **Front**: What is the past participle of "fly"?
*   **Back**: **flown**. (fly $\rightarrow$ flew $\rightarrow$ flown).
