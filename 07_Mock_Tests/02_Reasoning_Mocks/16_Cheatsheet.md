# Reasoning Ability - Cheatsheet

Quick reference sheet of rules and guidelines for Reasoning Ability.

---

## ⚡ Reasoning Cheatsheet

### 1. Seating Arrangement
*   **Linear**: Facing North: Left is towards left hand, Right is towards right hand. Facing South: Left is towards right, Right is towards left.
*   **Circular**:
    *   Facing Center: Left is Clockwise (CW), Right is Counter-Clockwise (CCW).
    *   Facing Outside: Left is Counter-Clockwise (CCW), Right is Clockwise (CW).

### 2. Blood Relations Symbols
*   Male: `[+]` or Square.
*   Female: `[-]` or Circle.
*   Siblings: Single horizontal link (`A - B`).
*   Spouses: Double horizontal link (`A = B`).
*   Generations: Vertical link (`Parent | Child`).

### 3. Coding-Decoding
*   Use `EJOTY` to remember letter rankings (5, 10, 15, 20, 25).
*   Opposite Letters rule: Sum of ranks of opposite letters is always 27 (e.g. A (1) + Z (26) = 27, B (2) + Y (25) = 27).

### 4. Clocks & Calendars
*   Angle between hands at $H$ hours and $M$ minutes:
    $$\text{Angle} = \left| 30H - 5.5M \right|^\circ$$
*   Leap year: Divisible by 4, but century years must be divisible by 400.
*   Number of odd days:
    *   Ordinary year: 1 odd day.
    *   Leap year: 2 odd days.
