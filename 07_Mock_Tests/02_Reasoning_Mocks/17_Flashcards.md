## 📇 Flashcard Deck

### Card 1: Clock Hands Angle
*   **Front**: What is the angle between clock hands at 8:20?
*   **Back**: Here $H=8, M=20$.
    $$\text{Angle} = |30(8) - 5.5(20)| = |240 - 110| = 130^\circ$$

### Card 2: Opposite Letters
*   **Front**: What is the opposite letter of 'G'?
*   **Back**: 'G' has rank 7. Opposite rank $= 27 - 7 = 20$. Rank 20 is 'T'. **T**. (Hint: GT Road).

### Card 3: Odd Days
*   **Front**: How many odd days are there in 100 years?
*   **Back**: 100 years contains 76 ordinary years and 24 leap years.
    $$\text{Odd days} = 76 \times 1 + 24 \times 2 = 124 \text{ days}$$
    $124 \div 7 = 17$ weeks and **5 odd days**.

### Card 4: Syllogisms
*   **Front**: If "Some A are B" and "Some B are C", can we conclude "Some A are C"?
*   **Back**: **No**. No direct connection between A and C is guaranteed.

### Card 5: Seating arrangement
*   **Front**: If facing center, is "to the right of X" clockwise or counter-clockwise?
*   **Back**: **Counter-clockwise**.
