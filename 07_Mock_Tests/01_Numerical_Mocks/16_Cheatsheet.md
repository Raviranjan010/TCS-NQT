# Numerical Ability - Cheatsheet

Quick reference sheet of speed formulas and tricks for the TCS NQT exam.

---

## ⚡ Speed Tricks Cheatsheet

### 1. Percentage & Successive Changes
*   Successive Changes $A\%$ and $B\%$:
    $$\text{Net Change} = A + B + \frac{A \cdot B}{100}$$
    *(Use negative sign for decrease/discount)*

### 2. Time & Work Rules
*   If A does work in $x$ days and B in $y$ days, together they take:
    $$\text{Time} = \frac{x \cdot y}{x + y} \text{ days}$$
*   If A, B, C take $x, y, z$ days respectively, together they take:
    $$\text{Time} = \frac{x \cdot y \cdot z}{xy + yz + zx} \text{ days}$$

### 3. Time Speed Distance
*   $\text{1 km/h} = \frac{5}{18} \text{ m/s}$
*   $\text{1 m/s} = \frac{18}{5} \text{ km/h}$
*   Average Speed (equal distance split):
    $$\text{Avg Speed} = \frac{2 \cdot v_1 \cdot v_2}{v_1 + v_2}$$

### 4. Permutations & Combinations
*   Circular Permutation: $(N-1)!$
*   Number of diagonals in a polygon of $N$ sides:
    $$\text{Diagonals} = \frac{N(N-3)}{2}$$

### 5. Probability Basics
*   $\text{Probability} = \frac{\text{Favorable Outcomes}}{\text{Total Outcomes}}$
*   Odds in favor of event $E = a:b \implies P(E) = \frac{a}{a+b}$.
