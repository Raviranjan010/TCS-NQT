# Data Interpretation - Pattern Recognition Guide

## 1. The Ratio Comparison Pattern
*   **Trigger Phrasing:** *"In which of the following years was the ratio of exports to imports highest?"*
*   **Strategy:** Write fractions as decimals or check which numerator has the largest margin over its denominator.

### Immediate Worked Example:
Identify which ratio is highest: $12/15, 18/20, 14/19$.
*   **Decimals:** $12/15 = 0.80$, $18/20 = 0.90$, $14/19 \approx 0.73$.
*   **Highest:** $18/20$ ($0.90$).
