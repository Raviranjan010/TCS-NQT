# Data Interpretation - Solved Examples

## 🟢 Easy-Level Examples
### Example 1. Simple table reading
*   **Question:** Based on the table, find the average production of cars by company X over 3 years: Year 1 = 150, Year 2 = 180, Year 3 = 210.
*   **Solution:**
    $$\text{Average} = \frac{150 + 180 + 210}{3} = 180$$

## 🟡 Medium-Level Examples
### Example 2. Pie Chart Budgeting
*   **Question:** A pie chart shows family expenses: Rent $= 30\%$, Food $= 25\%$, Others $= 45\%$. If the total budget is ₹40,000, find the expenditure on Rent and Food combined.
*   **Solution:**
    *   Combined percentage $= 30\% + 25\% = 55\%$.
    *   Combined expenditure $= 40000 \times 0.55 = ₹22,000$.

## 🔴 Hard-Level Examples
### Example 3. Mixed graphs percentage share
*   **Question:** A bar graph shows total production of crops (Wheat + Rice) in 2024 as 500 tons. A pie chart shows Wheat is $40\%$ of the total crops. If crop production grows by $20\%$ in 2025 and Wheat's share increases to $45\%$, find the production of Rice in 2025.
*   **Solution:**
    *   Total crop in 2025 $= 500 \times 1.20 = 600$ tons.
    *   Wheat in 2025 $= 45\% \text{ of } 600 = 270$ tons.
    *   Rice in 2025 $= 600 - 270 = 330$ tons.
