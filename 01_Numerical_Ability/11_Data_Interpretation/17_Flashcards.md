# Data Interpretation - Flashcards

*   **Card 1: What does a pie chart sector represent?**
    *   *Answer:* The proportion of a single sub-category relative to the whole dataset.
*   **Card 2: How to convert pie degrees to percent?**
    *   *Answer:* Divide the degrees by 3.6.
*   **Card 3: What is the formula for percentage increase?**
    *   *Answer:* $\frac{\text{New Value} - \text{Old Value}}{\text{Old Value}} \times 100$.
*   **Card 4: How to interpret mixed graphs?**
    *   *Answer:* Read total metrics from the primary graph (e.g., bar chart) and apply percentage/ratio distribution splits from the secondary graph (e.g., pie chart).
