# Data Interpretation - Past Year Questions (PYQs)

### PYQ 1. Export-Import Ratio Analysis
*   **Question:** The imports and exports of a company (in ₹ Crore) are given. If imports in 2024 were ₹250 Crore and exports were ₹300 Crore, find the percentage by which exports exceeded imports.
*   **Pattern ID:** DI_EXP_IMP_PYQ
*   **Approach:** Calculate $\frac{\text{Export} - \text{Import}}{\text{Import}} \times 100$.
*   **Solution:**
    $$\text{Percentage} = \frac{300 - 250}{250} \times 100 = \frac{50}{250} \times 100 = 20\%$$
*   **Shortcut:** $50$ is $1/5$ of $250$, and $1/5 = 20\%$.
*   **Variation & Trap:** Do not use exports as the denominator base.
