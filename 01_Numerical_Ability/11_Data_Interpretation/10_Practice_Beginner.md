# Data Interpretation - Practice Beginner

**Q1.** A company's revenue is ₹200M in Year 1 and ₹240M in Year 2. Find percentage increase.
> 🎯 Hint: Change in revenue divided by Year 1 revenue.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** Increase $= (40 / 200) \times 100 = 20\%$.
*   **Answer:** 20%
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐☆☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 15s
</details>
