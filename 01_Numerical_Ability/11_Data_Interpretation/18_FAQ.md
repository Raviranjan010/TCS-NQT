# Data Interpretation - FAQ

**Q1. How can I speed up DI calculations?**
*   **Answer:** Memorize fraction-to-percentage conversions and use estimation for options elimination rather than doing full long division.

**Q2. What is CAGR?**
*   **Answer:** Compound Annual Growth Rate, representing the geometric growth rate of a value over multiple time periods.

**Q3. What are common traps in bar charts?**
*   **Answer:** Checking if the vertical axis starts at 0 (non-zero baselines distort visual comparisons) and ensuring correct readings of stacked vs clustered bars.

**Q4. What is caselet DI?**
*   **Answer:** A paragraph of text containing data points in narrative form, requiring you to structure it into a table before solving questions.
