# Data Interpretation - Practice Advanced

**Q1.** In a mixed bar chart, the sales of product A and B in 2024 are in the ratio $3:2$. In 2025, sales of A increased by $10\%$ and sales of B decreased by $20\%$. Find the new ratio of sales of A to B.
> 🎯 Hint: Write the initial values as $300$ and $200$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:**
    *   Initial: A $= 300$, B $= 200$.
    *   New A $= 300 \times 1.10 = 330$.
    *   New B $= 200 \times 0.80 = 160$.
    *   New Ratio $= 330 : 160 = 33 : 16$.
*   **Answer:** 33:16
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 45s
</details>
