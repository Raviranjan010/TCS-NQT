# Data Interpretation - Timed Practice

**Q1.** A pie chart has 4 sectors: A ($30\%$), B ($20\%$), C ($40\%$), and D. Find the angle of sector D.
> 🎯 Hint: Sum of all sectors must equal $100\%$ and $360^\circ$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:**
    *   Percentage of D $= 100 - (30 + 20 + 40) = 10\%$.
    *   Angle of D $= 10\% \times 3.6 = 36^\circ$.
*   **Answer:** $36^\circ$
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 25s
</details>
