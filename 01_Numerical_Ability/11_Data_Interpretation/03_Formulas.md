# Data Interpretation - Formulas & Derivations

### 1. Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) Approximation
$$\text{Simple Avg Growth} = \frac{\text{End Value} - \text{Start Value}}{\text{Start Value} \times \text{Years}} \times 100$$
*   **Worked Example:** Revenue grows from ₹100M to ₹150M over 5 years. Find the average annual growth.
    $$\text{Rate} = \frac{150 - 100}{100 \times 5} \times 100 = 10\% \text{ per year}$$
