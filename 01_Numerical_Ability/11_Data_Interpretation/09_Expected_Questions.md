# 11 Data Interpretation - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

**Q1.** Based on a production bar chart, the output in 2024 was 120 tons and in 2025 was 175 tons. Find the percentage increase in production from 2024 to 2025.
*   **Type:** DI - Percentage Change Calculation
*   **Answer:** Percentage Increase = ((2025 Output - 2024 Output) * 100) / 2024 Output = ((175 - 120) * 100) / 120 = 45.83%.
*   **Time target:** 45 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 90% of papers)
