# Data Interpretation - Expected Questions

### Question 1. Pie chart degree distribution
*   **Question:** A pie chart representing a city's population shows Sector A has an angle of $108^\circ$. What percentage of the population resides in Sector A?
*   **Solution:**
    $$\text{Percentage} = \frac{108}{360} \times 100 = \frac{3}{10} \times 100 = 30\%$$
