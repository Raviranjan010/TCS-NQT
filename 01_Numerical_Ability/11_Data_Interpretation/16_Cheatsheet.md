# Data Interpretation - Cheatsheet

*   **Percentage Increase/Decrease:**
    *   $\text{Change \%} = \frac{\text{Difference}}{\text{Original Base}} \times 100$
*   **Pie Chart Conversion Formulas:**
    *   $\text{Degrees} = \text{Percentage} \times 3.6$
    *   $\text{Percentage} = \frac{\text{Degrees}}{3.6}$
*   **Average Calculation:**
    *   $\text{Average} = \frac{\text{Sum}}{\text{Count}}$
*   **Ratio Representation:**
    *   $\text{Ratio of A to B} = \frac{\text{Value of A}}{\text{Value of B}}$
