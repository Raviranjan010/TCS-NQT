# Data Interpretation - Practice Intermediate

**Q1.** The ratio of male to female employees in a firm is $5:3$. If there are 120 male employees, find the total number of employees.
> 🎯 Hint: 5 parts correspond to 120. Find 8 parts.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** 1 part $= 120 / 5 = 24$. Total parts $= 5+3 = 8$. Total employees $= 8 \times 24 = 192$.
*   **Answer:** 192
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 35s
</details>
