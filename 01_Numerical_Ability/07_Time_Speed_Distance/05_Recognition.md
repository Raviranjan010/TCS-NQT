# Time Speed Distance - Pattern Recognition Guide

## 1. Late-Early Pattern
*   **Trigger Phrasing:** *"If I travel at speed S1, I am late by L minutes. If I travel at S2, I am early by E minutes. Find distance."*
*   **Strategy:** Distance $D = \frac{S_1 \times S_2}{|S_1 - S_2|} \times \text{Difference in Time}$.

### Immediate Worked Example:
Walking at 4 km/hr, a student is 10 min late. At 5 km/hr, he is 5 min early. Find distance.
*   **Calculation:** Time difference $= 10 - (-5) = 15\text{ min} = 0.25\text{ hours}$.
    $$D = \frac{4 \times 5}{5 - 4} \times 0.25 = 20 \times 0.25 = 5\text{ km}$$
