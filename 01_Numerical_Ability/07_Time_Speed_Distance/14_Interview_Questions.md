# Time Speed Distance - Interview Questions

**Q1.** How is relative speed applied in communication protocols like TCP flow control or collision detection?
*   **Answer:** Just as two trains closing in on each other reduce space at $S_1 + S_2$, data packets traveling from source to destination and acknowledgments returning in opposite directions close their round-trip distance relative to their transmission rates, allowing congestion control algorithms to compute network latency (RTT).
