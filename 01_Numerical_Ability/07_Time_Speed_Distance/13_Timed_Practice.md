# Time Speed Distance - Timed Practice

**Q1.** Two cars start at the same time from A and B, 120 km apart. If they travel towards each other, they meet in 1 hour. If they travel in the same direction, they meet in 6 hours. Find speed of faster car.
> 🎯 Hint: $S_1 + S_2 = 120/1$ and $S_1 - S_2 = 120/6$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:**
    *   $S_1 + S_2 = 120$
    *   $S_1 - S_2 = 20 \implies 2S_1 = 140 \implies S_1 = 70$ km/hr.
*   **Answer:** 70 km/hr
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 45s
</details>
