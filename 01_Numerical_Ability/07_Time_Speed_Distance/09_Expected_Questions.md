# Time Speed Distance - Expected Questions

### Question 1. Upstream and Downstream trip
*   **Question:** A boat goes 24 km downstream in 2 hours and 24 km upstream in 6 hours. Find stream speed.
*   **Solution:**
    *   Downstream rate $D = 24 / 2 = 12$ km/hr.
    *   Upstream rate $U_{up} = 24 / 6 = 4$ km/hr.
    *   Stream speed $V = (D - U_{up})/2 = (12 - 4)/2 = 4$ km/hr.
