# 07 Time Speed Distance - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

---

**Q1.** A car travels from city X to city Y at a speed of 45 km/h and returns at a speed of 65 km/h. Find the average speed of the car for the entire journey.
*   **Type:** Average Speed for Equal Distance
*   **Answer:** Average Speed = (2 * S1 * S2) / (S1 + S2) = (2 * 45 * 65) / (45 + 65) = 53.18 km/h.
*   **Time target:** 30 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 80% of papers)
