# Time Speed Distance - Practice Intermediate

**Q1.** Excluding stoppages, a bus travels at 54 km/hr, and including stoppages, it travels at 45 km/hr. For how many minutes does the bus stop per hour?
> 🎯 Hint: Time of stoppage per hour $= \frac{\text{Difference in speed}}{\text{Faster Speed}} \times 60$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:**
    $$\text{Stoppage Time} = \frac{54 - 45}{54} \times 60 = \frac{9}{54} \times 60 = \frac{1}{6} \times 60 = 10\text{ minutes/hour}$$
*   **Answer:** 10 minutes
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 40s
</details>
