# Time Speed Distance - Practice Advanced

**Q1.** A man can row 6 km/hr in still water. If the speed of the current is 2 km/hr, it takes him 3 hours to row to a place and come back. How far is the place?
> 🎯 Hint: Total time is the sum of upstream time and downstream time.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Approach:** Equation setup.
*   **Step-by-step:**
    *   Downstream Speed $= 6 + 2 = 8$ km/hr.
    *   Upstream Speed $= 6 - 2 = 4$ km/hr.
    *   Let distance be $d$.
    *   $\frac{d}{8} + \frac{d}{4} = 3 \implies \frac{3d}{8} = 3 \implies d = 8\text{ km}$.
*   **Answer:** 8 km
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ | **Target Time:** 60s
</details>
