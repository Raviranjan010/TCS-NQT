# Time Speed Distance - Past Year Questions (PYQs)

### PYQ 1. Train passing another train
*   **Question:** Two trains of lengths 150m and 120m are running on parallel tracks. If they run in opposite directions at 42 km/hr and 30 km/hr respectively, how long to cross each other?
*   **Pattern ID:** TSD_TRAIN_CROSS_PYQ
*   **Approach:** Total distance $= L_1 + L_2$. Relative speed in opposite direction $= S_1 + S_2$.
*   **Solution:**
    *   Distance $= 150 + 120 = 270$ meters.
    *   Relative speed $= 42 + 30 = 72$ km/hr $= 72 \times (5/18) = 20$ m/s.
    *   Time $= 270 / 20 = 13.5$ seconds.
*   **Shortcut:** $72$ km/hr is exactly $20$ m/s. $270 / 20 = 13.5$.
*   **Variation & Trap:** If running in the same direction, relative speed is $42 - 30 = 12$ km/hr.
