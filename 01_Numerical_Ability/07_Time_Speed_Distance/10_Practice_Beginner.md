# Time Speed Distance - Practice Beginner

**Q1.** Convert 54 km/hr to m/s.
> 🎯 Hint: Multiply by $5/18$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** $54 \times 5 / 18 = 3 \times 5 = 15$ m/s.
*   **Answer:** 15 m/s
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐☆☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 10s
</details>
