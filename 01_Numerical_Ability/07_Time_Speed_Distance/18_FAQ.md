# Time Speed Distance - FAQ

**Q1. Why does average speed not equal the simple average of speeds?**
*   **Answer:** Because the body spends more time traveling at the slower speed than at the faster speed.

**Q2. What is the escalator concept?**
*   **Answer:** It is identical to boats and streams, where the person's walking speed represents boat speed and the escalator movement represents stream speed.

**Q3. How do we solve late-early arrival problems?**
*   **Answer:** Calculate the time difference ($T_{diff} = T_{late} + T_{early}$ if opposite, or $|T_1 - T_2|$ if same) and use $D = \frac{S_1 S_2}{|S_1 - S_2|} \times T_{diff}$.

**Q4. What is average speed?**
*   **Answer:** Total distance traveled divided by total time taken to travel that distance.
