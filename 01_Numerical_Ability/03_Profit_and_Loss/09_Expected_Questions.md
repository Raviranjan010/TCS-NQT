# 03 Profit and Loss - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

---

**Q1.** A retailer buys an article for Rs.450 and spends Rs.5 on transportation. He sells it for Rs.560. Find his profit or loss percentage.
*   **Type:** Profit/Loss Percentage with Overhead Expenses
*   **Answer:** Total Cost Price = 450 + 5 = 455. Selling Price = 560. Profit = 560 - 455 = 105. Profit % = (105 * 100) / 455 = 23.08%
*   **Time target:** 45 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 75% of papers)
