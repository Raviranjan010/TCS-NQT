# 02 Percentage - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

---

**Q1.** If the price of petrol increases by 15% (from Rs.100 to Rs.115 per liter), by what percentage must a consumer reduce consumption to keep the total expenditure constant?
*   **Type:** Percentage Increase and Decrease
*   **Answer:** Required reduction = (increase * 100) / (100 + increase) = (15 * 100) / (100 + 15) = 13.04%
*   **Time target:** 40 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 85% of papers)
