# ⚡ Percentage Cheatsheet (Weight: 2–3 Questions)

---

## 📊 Formulas & Memory Tricks

| Formula Name | Formula Equation | Memory Trick |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Percentage Change** | $\frac{\text{Change}}{\text{Original}} \times 100$ | *"Divide change by the start, to be smart."* |
| **Successive Change (2 elements)**| $x + y + \frac{xy}{100}\%$ | *"Sum, product, divide, add with pride."* |
| **Base Adjustment (Increase)** | $\frac{x}{100+x} \times 100\%$ | *"If top goes up, add it to the cup (denominator)."* |
| **Base Adjustment (Decrease)** | $\frac{x}{100-x} \times 100\%$ | *"If top goes down, subtract it from the town."* |

---

## ⚡ Shortcuts
*   **Commutative Swap**: $A\%$ of $B = B\%$ of $A$. Swap them when $B$ is a clean number like 50, 25, or 20.
*   **The Splitting Rule**: Find $56\%$ of $X$ by summing $50\% + 5\% + 1\%$ of $X$.
*   **Fraction Jump Scale**: If A rises by $\frac{1}{x}$, B must decrease by $\frac{1}{x+1}$ to keep the product constant.
*   **Equal percentage swings**: A price change of $+P\%$ followed by $-P\%$ always leads to a net decrease of $(P/10)^2\%$.

---

## ⚠️ Warning: Top 3 Traps
> [!WARNING]
> **Trap 1: Successive Summation**
> Never add successive changes directly ($10\% - 10\% \neq 0\%$). Always use $x + y + xy/100$ or multiplying factors.
> 
> **Trap 2: Cost Price Discount**
> Never calculate discounts on the Cost Price (CP). Discounts are strictly calculated on the Marked Price (MP).
> 
> **Trap 3: Symbol typing on NQT**
> Do not type `%` or `rs` in the whole-number input box during the exam. Enter ONLY numeric digits (e.g. `20`, not `20%`).
