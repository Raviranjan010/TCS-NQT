# Timed Practice: Percentages

**Time Limit**: 12 Minutes  
**Questions**: 10  
**Format**: MCQ & FIB

---

## 📝 Part 1: Question Booklet

1. If $A$'s salary is $20\%$ more than $B$'s, by what percentage is $B$'s salary less than $A$'s? (MCQ: A. $20\%$, B. $16.67\%$, C. $25\%$, D. $15\%$)
2. Price of sugar increases by $20\%$. By what percentage must consumption drop to maintain budget? (FIB)
3. Walking at $4\text{ km/h}$, a student is 10 min late. At $5\text{ km/h}$, he is 5 min early. Find distance. (FIB)
4. A salesman gets $8\%$ commission on sales. If total sales are $50000$, find commission. (FIB)
5. Population increases by $10\%$ in year 1 and decreases by $10\%$ in year 2. Net change? (MCQ: A. No change, B. $1\%$ decrease, C. $1\%$ increase, D. $2\%$ decrease)
6. A student secures $35\%$ marks and fails by 15 marks. If max marks is 400, find pass marks. (FIB)
7. Fresh fruit contains $80\%$ water and dry fruit contains $10\%$ water. How much dry fruit from $180\text{ kg}$ fresh fruit? (FIB)
8. In an election, winner gets $58\%$ of valid votes and wins by $800$ votes. Find total valid votes. (FIB)
9. If numerator increases by $200\%$ and denominator increases by $150\%$, fraction is $6/5$. Find original fraction. (MCQ: A. $1/1$, B. $2/3$, C. $1/2$, D. $3/4$)
10. A spends $30\%$ on food, $40\%$ of remaining on rent, and saves $2100$. Find total salary. (FIB)

---

## 🔑 Part 2: Answer Key & Solutions

1. **Answer**: B ($16.67\%$).
   - Solution: $\frac{20}{120} \times 100 = 16.67\%$.
2. **Answer**: 16.67.
   - Solution: $20\% = 1/5 \text{ increase} \implies 1/6 = 16.67\% \text{ decrease}$.
3. **Answer**: 5.
   - Solution: $\text{Distance} = \frac{4 \times 5}{1} \times \frac{15}{60} = 5\text{ km}$.
4. **Answer**: 4000.
   - Solution: $50000 \times 0.08 = 4000$.
5. **Answer**: B ($1\%$ decrease).
   - Solution: $1.10 \times 0.90 = 0.99 \implies 1\%$ decrease.
6. **Answer**: 155.
   - Solution: Pass Marks $= 35\%$ of $400 + 15 = 140 + 15 = 155$.
7. **Answer**: 40.
   - Solution: Pulp weight $= 180 \times 0.20 = 36\text{ kg}$. Dry fruit weight $= 36 / 0.90 = 40\text{ kg}$.
8. **Answer**: 5000.
   - Solution: Difference $= 58\% - 42\% = 16\% = 800 \implies$ total $= 5000$.
9. **Answer**: A ($1/1$).
   - Solution: $\frac{x \times 3}{y \times 2.5} = 6/5 \implies \frac{x}{y} \times 1.2 = 1.2 \implies x/y = 1/1$.
10. **Answer**: 5000.
    - Solution: $x \times 0.70 \times 0.60 = 2100 \implies 0.42x = 2100 \implies x = 5000$.
