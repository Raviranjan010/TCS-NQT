### Comparison Table:

| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mutually Exclusive** | Cannot occur together | $P(A \cap B) = 0$ |
| **Independent** | Occurrence of one doesn't affect other | $P(A \cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B)$ |
