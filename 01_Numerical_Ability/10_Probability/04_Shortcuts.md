# Probability - Shortcuts & Speed Hacks

## ⚡ 1. "At Least One" Rule
*   **Shortcut:** $P(\text{At least one success}) = 1 - P(\text{All failures})$
*   **Real Number Demo:** If the probability of hitting a target is $0.2$, the probability of hitting it at least once in 3 attempts is $1 - (0.8)^3 = 1 - 0.512 = 0.488$.
