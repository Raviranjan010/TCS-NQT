# Probability - Common Mistakes & Traps

### Mistake 1. Forgetting to subtract intersection
*   **Incorrect:** Find $P(\text{King or Spade}) = P(\text{King}) + P(\text{Spade}) = 4/52 + 13/52 = 17/52$.
*   **Correct:** King of Spades was counted twice. Subtract it: $4/52 + 13/52 - 1/52 = 16/52 = 4/13$.
