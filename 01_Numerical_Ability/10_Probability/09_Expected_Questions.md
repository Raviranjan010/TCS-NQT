# Probability - Expected Questions

### Question 1. Leap year Sunday count
*   **Question:** Find the probability that a leap year contains 53 Sundays.
*   **Solution:**
    *   Leap year has 366 days $= 52$ weeks $+ 2$ extra days.
    *   These 2 days can be: (Mon, Tue), (Tue, Wed), (Wed, Thu), (Thu, Fri), (Fri, Sat), (Sat, Sun), (Sun, Mon).
    *   Total pairs $= 7$. Pairs with Sunday $= 2$.
    *   Probability $= 2/7$.
