# 10 Probability - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

**Q1.** A bag contains 4 red, 5 blue, and 6 green marbles. If a marble is drawn at random, what is the probability that it is NOT green?
*   **Type:** Basic Probability of Complementary Events
*   **Answer:** Total marbles = 15. Favorable marbles (not green) = 4 + 5 = 9. Probability = 9/15.
*   **Time target:** 30 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** Medium (appears in 55% of papers)
