# Probability - Practice Intermediate

**Q1.** A box contains 5 green and 7 yellow pens. If two pens are drawn at random without replacement, find the probability that both are green.
> 🎯 Hint: Use the multiplication rule for dependent events: $P(G_1) \times P(G_2 | G_1)$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:**
    $$P = \frac{5}{12} \times \frac{4}{11} = \frac{20}{132} = \frac{5}{33}$$
*   **Answer:** 5/33
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 45s
</details>
