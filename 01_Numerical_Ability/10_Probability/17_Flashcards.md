# Probability - Flashcards

*   **Card 1: What is the probability of a sure event?**
    *   *Answer:* 1.
*   **Card 2: What are mutually exclusive events?**
    *   *Answer:* Events that cannot occur at the same time ($P(A \cap B) = 0$).
*   **Card 3: How is conditional probability calculated?**
    *   *Answer:* $P(A|B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}$.
*   **Card 4: What is the sample space size for tossing 3 coins?**
    *   *Answer:* $2^3 = 8$ outcomes.
