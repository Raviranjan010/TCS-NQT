# Probability - Timed Practice

**Q1.** Two dice are rolled. Find the probability that the sum is a prime number.
> 🎯 Hint: List sums that are prime (2, 3, 5, 7, 11) and count their occurrences.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:**
    *   Sum 2: (1,1) $\rightarrow 1$
    *   Sum 3: (1,2), (2,1) $\rightarrow 2$
    *   Sum 5: (1,4), (4,1), (2,3), (3,2) $\rightarrow 4$
    *   Sum 7: 6 outcomes
    *   Sum 11: (5,6), (6,5) $\rightarrow 2$
    *   Total favorable $= 1 + 2 + 4 + 6 + 2 = 15$ outcomes.
    *   $P = 15/36 = 5/12$.
*   **Answer:** 5/12
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 50s
</details>
