# Probability - Past Year Questions (PYQs)

### PYQ 1. Coin arrangements probability
*   **Question:** Three unbiased coins are tossed. Find the probability of getting at least 2 heads.
*   **Pattern ID:** PROB_COIN_LEAST_PYQ
*   **Approach:** List elements or use Binomial.
*   **Solution:**
    *   Sample Space: $\{HHH, HHT, HTH, THH, TTH, THT, HTT, TTT\} \implies 8$ outcomes.
    *   At least 2 heads: $\{HHH, HHT, HTH, THH\} \implies 4$ outcomes.
    *   $P = 4/8 = 1/2$.
*   **Shortcut:** $1/2$ (symmetry of heads and tails).
*   **Variation & Trap:** Don't confuse "at least" with "at most".
