# Probability - Practice Beginner

**Q1.** What is the probability of drawing a red card from a standard deck?
> 🎯 Hint: There are 26 red cards in a deck of 52.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** $P = 26/52 = 1/2$.
*   **Answer:** 1/2
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐☆☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 10s
</details>
