# Time and Work - Solved Examples

## 🟢 Easy-Level Examples
### Example 1. Basic Pipes and Cisterns
*   **Question:** Pipe A can fill a tank in 6 hours and Pipe B can empty it in 12 hours. If both are open, how long to fill the tank?
*   **Solution:** Let total capacity $= 12$ units.
    *   Efficiency A $= +2$, B $= -1$ (emptying).
    *   Net $= +1$ unit/hour.
    *   Time $= 12 / 1 = 12$ hours.

## 🟡 Medium-Level Examples
### Example 2. Leaving before completion
*   **Question:** A can do a work in 10 days, B in 15 days. They start together but A leaves 2 days before the work is completed. Find total days.
*   **Solution:** Total work $= 30$ units. Eff: A $= 3$, B $= 2$.
    Let total time be $t$ days.
    A worked for $t-2$ days, B worked for $t$ days.
    $$3(t-2) + 2(t) = 30 \implies 5t - 6 = 30 \implies 5t = 36 \implies t = 7.2\text{ days}$$

## 🔴 Hard-Level Examples
### Example 3. Men, Women, and Children
*   **Question:** 3 men or 4 women can complete a work in 43 days. In how many days will 7 men and 5 women do it?
*   **Solution:**
    $$3M = 4W \implies M = \frac{4}{3}W$$
    Total work in terms of women $= 4W \times 43$.
    Target Group $= 7M + 5W = 7\left(\frac{4}{3}W\right) + 5W = \frac{43}{3}W$.
    Let days be $D$.
    $$\frac{43}{3}W \times D = 4W \times 43 \implies D = 12\text{ days}$$
