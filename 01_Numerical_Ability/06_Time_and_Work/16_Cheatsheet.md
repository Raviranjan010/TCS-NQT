# Time and Work - Cheatsheet

*   **Together Formula (2 people):** $T = \frac{xy}{x+y}$
*   **Together Formula (3 people):** $T = \frac{xyz}{xy+yz+zx}$
*   **Men-Days-Hours:** $\frac{M_1 D_1 H_1}{W_1} = \frac{M_2 D_2 H_2}{W_2}$
*   **Work Rate:** $W = R \times T$
*   **Pipes & Cisterns Net Flow:** $\text{Rate}_{net} = \frac{1}{\text{Inlet}} - \frac{1}{\text{Outlet}}$
