# Time and Work - Practice Intermediate

**Q1.** A is thrice as efficient as B and is therefore able to finish a job in 60 days less than B. Find the time in which they can do it together.
> 🎯 Hint: Let days of A be $x$ and B be $3x$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:**
    $$3x - x = 60 \implies 2x = 60 \implies x = 30\text{ (A's days)}$$
    B's days $= 90$.
    Together $= \frac{30 \times 90}{30 + 90} = \frac{2700}{120} = 22.5\text{ days}$.
*   **Answer:** 22.5 days
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 50s
</details>
