# Time and Work - Timed Practice

**Q1.** A and B can do a work in 12 days, B and C in 15 days, C and A in 20 days. How long will A, B, and C take working together?
> 🎯 Hint: Sum the rates to get $2(A+B+C)$ efficiency.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** Total work $= 60$ units.
    *   $A+B = 5$, $B+C = 4$, $C+A = 3$.
    *   $2(A+B+C) = 12 \implies A+B+C = 6$.
    *   Time taken $= 60 / 6 = 10$ days.
*   **Answer:** 10 days
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 45s
</details>
