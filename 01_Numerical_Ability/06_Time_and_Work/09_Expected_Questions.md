# 06 Time and Work - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

---

**Q1.** A can complete a piece of work in 12 days and B can do the same work in 18 days. In how many days can they complete the work together?
*   **Type:** Two Person Work Sharing
*   **Answer:** Together time = (A * B) / (A + B) = (12 * 18) / (12 + 18) = 7.2 days.
*   **Time target:** 30 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 90% of papers)
