# Time and Work - Expected Questions

### Question 1. Pipes with leakage
*   **Question:** An inlet pipe can fill a pool in 8 hours. Due to a leak at the bottom, it takes 10 hours. How long will the leak take to empty the full pool?
*   **Solution:** Capacity $= 40$. Inlet rate $= 5$. Combined rate $= 4$.
    Leak rate $= 5 - 4 = 1$ unit/hour.
    Time $= 40 / 1 = 40$ hours.
