# Time and Work - Past Year Questions (PYQs)

### PYQ 1. Alternating work sequence
*   **Question:** A, B, and C can do a piece of work in 20, 30, and 60 days respectively. In how many days can A complete the work if he is assisted by B and C on every third day?
*   **Pattern ID:** TIME_WORK_ASSIST_PYQ
*   **Approach:** Calculate work done in a 3-day cycle: Day 1: A, Day 2: A, Day 3: A+B+C.
*   **Solution:** Let total work $= 60$ units.
    *   Efficiencies: A $= 3$, B $= 2$, C $= 1$.
    *   Day 1: A does $3$ units.
    *   Day 2: A does $3$ units.
    *   Day 3: A+B+C does $3+2+1 = 6$ units.
    *   3-Day Cycle Work $= 3 + 3 + 6 = 12$ units.
    *   To complete 60 units, we need $60/12 = 5$ cycles.
    *   Total days $= 5 \times 3 = 15$ days.
*   **Shortcut:** 3 days $= 12$ units. Multiply by 5 $\implies 15$ days.
*   **Variation & Trap:** Don't assume A, B, and C work on alternate days. The wording is A works daily, assisted on every 3rd day.
