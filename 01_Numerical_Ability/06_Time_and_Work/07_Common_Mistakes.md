# Time and Work - Common Mistakes & Traps

### Mistake 1. Adding times directly
*   **Incorrect:** A takes 5 days, B takes 10 days $\implies$ together they take $5+10=15$ days.
*   **Correct:** Working together must take *less* time than the fastest individual. Use rates: $1/5 + 1/10 = 3/10 \implies 3.33$ days.
