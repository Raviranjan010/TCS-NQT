# Time and Work - Practice Beginner

**Q1.** A can do work in 6 days, B in 12 days. How long together?
> 🎯 Hint: Use the product over sum formula $xy/(x+y)$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** $T = (6 \times 12) / (6 + 12) = 72 / 18 = 4$ days.
*   **Answer:** 4 days
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐☆☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 20s
</details>
