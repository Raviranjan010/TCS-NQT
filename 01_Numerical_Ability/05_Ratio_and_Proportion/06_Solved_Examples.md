# Ratio and Proportion - Solved Examples

## 🟢 Easy-Level Examples
### Example 1. Combining ratios
*   **Question:** If $A:B = 3:4$ and $B:C = 8:9$, find $A:C$.
*   **Solution:**
    $$\frac{A}{C} = \frac{A}{B} \times \frac{B}{C} = \frac{3}{4} \times \frac{8}{9} = \frac{2}{3} \implies A:C = 2:3$$

## 🟡 Medium-Level Examples
### Example 2. Age ratio problems
*   **Question:** The ratio of ages of A and B is $4:5$. In 6 years, the ratio becomes $5:6$. Find their present ages.
*   **Solution:** Let ages be $4x$ and $5x$.
    $$\frac{4x+6}{5x+6} = \frac{5}{6} \implies 24x + 36 = 25x + 30 \implies x = 6$$
    Ages are $24$ and $30$ years.

## 🔴 Hard-Level Examples
### Example 3. Compound Partnership
*   **Question:** A, B, and C enter into a partnership. A invests ₹4,000 for 12 months, B invests ₹6,000 for 8 months, and C invests ₹8,000 for 6 months. How should a profit of ₹3,600 be shared?
*   **Solution:**
    $$\text{Ratio} = (4000 \times 12) : (6000 \times 8) : (8000 \times 6) = 48 : 48 : 48 = 1 : 1 : 1$$
    Each gets ₹1,200.
