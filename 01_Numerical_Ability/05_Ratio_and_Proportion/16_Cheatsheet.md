# Ratio and Proportion - Cheatsheet

*   **Mean Proportional:** $b = \sqrt{ac}$
*   **Third Proportional:** $c = b^2/a$
*   **Fourth Proportional:** $d = bc/a$
*   **Componendo & Dividendo:** If $a/b = c/d \implies (a+b)/(a-b) = (c+d)/(c-d)$
*   **Alligation Rule:**
    $$\frac{\text{Quantity of Cheaper}}{\text{Quantity of Dearer}} = \frac{D_p - M_p}{M_p - C_p}$$
