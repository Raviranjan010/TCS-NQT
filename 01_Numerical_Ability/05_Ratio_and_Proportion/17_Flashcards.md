# Ratio and Proportion - Flashcards

*   **Card 1: What is continuous proportion?**
    *   *Answer:* Three terms $a, b, c$ are in continuous proportion if $a:b = b:c$, meaning $b^2 = ac$.
*   **Card 2: Define duplicate and triplicate ratios.**
    *   *Answer:* Duplicate ratio of $a:b$ is $a^2 : b^2$. Triplicate ratio is $a^3 : b^3$.
*   **Card 3: How is profit divided in a partnership when capitals are invested for different times?**
    *   *Answer:* Profit is shared in the ratio of the product of capital and duration of investment, i.e., $C_1 T_1 : C_2 T_2$.
*   **Card 4: What is the rule of mixtures?**
    *   *Answer:* A method to calculate weighted average ratios when combining two ingredients of different prices.
