# Ratio and Proportion - Timed Practice

**Q1.** If $a:b = 5:7$ and $b:c = 6:11$, find $a:b:c$.
> 🎯 Hint: Multiply top ratio by 6 and bottom by 7 to balance B.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** $a:b = 30:42$, $b:c = 42:77 \implies a:b:c = 30:42:77$.
*   **Answer:** $30:42:77$
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 30s
</details>
