# Ratio and Proportion - Interview Questions

**Q1.** What is the practical application of Compound Ratio and Inverse Ratio in business and coding?
*   **Answer:** Compound ratios are used in multi-variable business partnerships where investment capital and time span are combined to define equity. Inverse ratios are used in physics and computing resource scheduling (e.g., speed and execution time are inversely proportional for a constant task workload).
