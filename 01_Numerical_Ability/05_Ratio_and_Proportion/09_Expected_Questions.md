# Ratio and Proportion - Expected Questions

### Question 1. Income-Expenditure Ratio
*   **Question:** The incomes of A and B are in the ratio $9:4$ and their expenditures are in the ratio $7:3$. If each saves ₹2,000, find their incomes.
*   **Solution:** Let incomes be $9x, 4x$ and expenditures $7y, 3y$.
    $$9x - 7y = 2000, \quad 4x - 3y = 2000$$
    Multiply first by 3 and second by 7:
    $$27x - 21y = 6000, \quad 28x - 21y = 14000 \implies x = 8000$$
    Incomes are ₹72,000 and ₹32,000.
