# Ratio and Proportion - Practice Intermediate

**Q1.** Salaries of A, B, and C are in the ratio $2:3:5$. If they receive increments of $15\%$, $10\%$, and $20\%$ respectively, what is the new ratio of their salaries?
> 🎯 Hint: Multiply each ratio term by the multiplier factor $(1 + \text{increment})$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** Let salaries be 200, 300, 500.
    *   New A $= 200 \times 1.15 = 230$
    *   New B $= 300 \times 1.10 = 330$
    *   New C $= 500 \times 1.20 = 600$
    *   Ratio $= 23 : 33 : 60$.
*   **Answer:** $23 : 33 : 60$
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 45s
</details>
