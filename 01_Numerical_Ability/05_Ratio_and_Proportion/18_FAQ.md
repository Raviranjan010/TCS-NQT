# Ratio and Proportion - FAQ

**Q1. What is the difference between ratio and proportion?**
*   **Answer:** A ratio compares two quantities, while a proportion is an equation stating that two ratios are equal.

**Q2. How do we balance A:B and B:C?**
*   **Answer:** Multiply the terms of one or both ratios so that the term corresponding to B becomes equal in both ratios.

**Q3. What is mean proportional?**
*   **Answer:** For two numbers $a$ and $c$, the mean proportional is $b = \sqrt{ac}$.

**Q4. What is partnership in math?**
*   **Answer:** A business association of two or more persons who invest their funds to run a business and share profits proportionally.
