# Ratio and Proportion - Formulas & Derivations

### 1. Mean Proportional Derivation
If $a, b, c$ are in continuous proportion:
$$\frac{a}{b} = \frac{b}{c} \implies b^2 = ac \implies b = \sqrt{ac}$$
*   **Worked Example:** Find the mean proportional of 4 and 9.
    $$b = \sqrt{4 \times 9} = 6$$

### 2. Partnership Profits
$$\text{Profit Ratio} = (\text{Investment}_1 \times \text{Time}_1) : (\text{Investment}_2 \times \text{Time}_2)$$
*   **Worked Example:** A invests ₹2,000 for 6 months, B invests ₹3,000 for 4 months.
    $$\text{Profit Ratio} = (2000 \times 6) : (3000 \times 4) = 12000 : 12000 = 1 : 1$$
