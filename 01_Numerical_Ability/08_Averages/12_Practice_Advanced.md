# Averages - Practice Advanced

**Q1.** The average of 11 numbers is 50. If the average of the first 6 numbers is 49 and that of the last 6 numbers is 52, find the 6th number.
> 🎯 Hint: The 6th number is counted twice in the two sub-groups.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:**
    *   Sum of 11 numbers $= 11 \times 50 = 550$.
    *   Sum of first 6 numbers $= 6 \times 49 = 294$.
    *   Sum of last 6 numbers $= 6 \times 52 = 312$.
    *   Sum of first 6 + last 6 $= 294 + 312 = 606$.
    *   6th number $= 606 - 550 = 56$.
*   **Answer:** 56
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ | **Target Time:** 60s
</details>
