# Averages - Timed Practice

**Q1.** The average age of 8 men is increased by 2 years when two of them whose ages are 21 and 23 years are replaced by two new men. Find the average age of the two new men.
> 🎯 Hint: Total age increases by $8 \times 2 = 16$ years.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:**
    *   Sum of ages of excluded men $= 21 + 23 = 44$.
    *   Sum of ages of new men $= 44 + (8 \times 2) = 60$.
    *   Average age of new men $= 60 / 2 = 30$ years.
*   **Answer:** 30 years
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 45s
</details>
