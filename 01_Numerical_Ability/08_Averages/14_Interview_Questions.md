# Averages - Interview Questions

**Q1.** What is the statistical difference between Mean, Median, and Mode, and when is Median preferred?
*   **Answer:** Mean is the arithmetic average, Median is the middle value of sorted data, and Mode is the most frequent value. Median is preferred when the dataset contains extreme outliers (e.g., salary distributions), as the mean is highly sensitive to outliers whereas the median is robust.
