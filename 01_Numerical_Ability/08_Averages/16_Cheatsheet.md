# Averages - Cheatsheet

*   **Average Formula:** $\text{Avg} = \frac{\text{Sum}}{\text{Count}}$
*   **Weighted Average:** $A_{w} = \frac{n_1 A_1 + n_2 A_2}{n_1 + n_2}$
*   **Average of first N natural numbers:** $\frac{N+1}{2}$
*   **Average of first N even numbers:** $N+1$
*   **Average of first N odd numbers:** $N$
*   **Average of first N squares:** $\frac{(N+1)(2N+1)}{6}$
*   **Replacement Value:** $\text{New} = \text{Old} + (\text{Count} \times \text{Avg Increase})$
