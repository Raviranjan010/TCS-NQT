# Averages - Solved Examples

## 🟢 Easy-Level Examples
### Example 1. Basic average with additions
*   **Question:** The average of 5 numbers is 20. If a 6th number is added, the average becomes 22. Find the 6th number.
*   **Solution:**
    *   Sum of 5 numbers $= 5 \times 20 = 100$.
    *   Sum of 6 numbers $= 6 \times 22 = 132$.
    *   6th number $= 132 - 100 = 32$.

## 🟡 Medium-Level Examples
### Example 2. Average age exclusions
*   **Question:** Average age of 24 students and their teacher is 15 years. If the teacher's age is excluded, the average age decreases by 1 year. Find the teacher's age.
*   **Solution:**
    *   Total count $= 25$. Sum $= 25 \times 15 = 375$.
    *   New count $= 24$. New Avg $= 14$. Sum $= 24 \times 14 = 336$.
    *   Teacher's age $= 375 - 336 = 39$ years.

## 🔴 Hard-Level Examples
### Example 3. Cricket batting average
*   **Question:** A batsman has an average of 40 runs in 30 innings. In the next innings, he scores 122 runs, thereby increasing his average. How many runs must he score in the 32nd innings to make the average 43?
*   **Solution:**
    *   Total runs in 30 innings $= 30 \times 40 = 1200$.
    *   Runs in 31 innings $= 1200 + 122 = 1322$.
    *   To get average of 43 in 32 innings, total runs needed $= 32 \times 43 = 1376$.
    *   Runs in 32nd innings $= 1376 - 1322 = 54$.
