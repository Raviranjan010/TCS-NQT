# Averages - Formulas & Derivations

### 1. Replacement of a Person
$$\text{New Value} = \text{Excluded Value} + (\text{Change in Average} \times \text{New Count})$$
*   **Derivation:** Let sum be $S$, count $N$. If $X$ is replaced by $Y$:
    $$S - X + Y = N(A + d) \implies S - X + Y = NA + Nd$$
    Since $S = NA$, we get:
    $$-X + Y = Nd \implies Y = X + Nd$$
*   **Worked Example:** The average weight of 10 people increases by 1.5 kg when one person weighing 50 kg is replaced. Find the weight of the new person.
    $$\text{Weight} = 50 + (1.5 \times 10) = 50 + 15 = 65\text{ kg}$$
