# Averages - Expected Questions

### Question 1. Weighted Average of Salaries
*   **Question:** The average salary of all workers in a workshop is ₹8,000. The average salary of 7 technicians is ₹12,000 and that of the rest is ₹6,000. Find the total number of workers.
*   **Solution:** Let total workers be $7 + x$.
    $$12000(7) + 6000(x) = 8000(7 + x) \implies 84000 + 6000x = 56000 + 8000x$$
    $$28000 = 2000x \implies x = 14$$
    Total $= 7 + 14 = 21$ workers.
