# 08 Averages - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

---

**Q1.** The average weight of 6 students is 51 kg. If the teacher's weight is added, the average increases by 2 kg. Find the weight of the teacher.
*   **Type:** Average with Inclusion / Exclusion
*   **Answer:** Teacher's Weight = New Total Weight - Old Total Weight = (7 * 53) - (6 * 51) = 65 kg.
*   **Time target:** 40 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 75% of papers)
