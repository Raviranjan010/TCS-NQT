# Averages - Practice Intermediate

**Q1.** The average weight of a class of 24 students is 35 kg. If the weight of the teacher is added, the average weight increases by 400g. Find the weight of the teacher.
> 🎯 Hint: Use the addition formula: $\text{New Value} = \text{Old Avg} + \text{New Count} \times \text{Increase}$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** Old Avg $= 35$, New Count $= 25$, Increase $= 0.4$ kg.
    $$\text{Teacher Weight} = 35 + (25 \times 0.4) = 35 + 10 = 45\text{ kg}$$
*   **Answer:** 45 kg
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 40s
</details>
