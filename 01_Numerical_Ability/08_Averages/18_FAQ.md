# Averages - FAQ

**Q1. What is the deviation method?**
*   **Answer:** A mental math shortcut where you assume a benchmark mean, compute differences (deviations) of all values from it, average those deviations, and add the result back to the benchmark.

**Q2. How to find average of consecutive odd numbers?**
*   **Answer:** For an odd count of numbers, it is the middle number. For an even count, it is the average of the two middle numbers.

**Q3. How does replacement affect total sum?**
*   **Answer:** The total sum changes by the net difference between the incoming value and the outgoing value: $\Delta S = Y_{in} - X_{out}$.

**Q4. What is weighted average?**
*   **Answer:** An average where each value is multiplied by a weight reflecting its relative importance or frequency before summing.
