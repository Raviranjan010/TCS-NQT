# Averages - Past Year Questions (PYQs)

### PYQ 1. Average of consecutive groups
*   **Question:** The average of 7 consecutive numbers is 20. The largest of these numbers is:
*   **Pattern ID:** AVG_CONSEC_PYQ
*   **Approach:** Middle number is the average.
*   **Solution:**
    *   With 7 terms, the 4th term is the average $= 20$.
    *   Numbers: 17, 18, 19, [20], 21, 22, 23.
    *   Largest $= 23$.
*   **Shortcut:** Largest $= \text{Average} + \frac{N-1}{2} = 20 + 3 = 23$.
*   **Variation & Trap:** Ensure to check if the question specifies consecutive *even* or *odd* numbers (where terms increase by 2 instead of 1).
