# Averages - Pattern Recognition Guide

## 1. Consecutives Average Pattern
*   **Trigger Phrasing:** *"The average of N consecutive odd/even/natural numbers is A. Find largest/smallest."*
*   **Strategy:** For consecutive terms, the average is the exact middle term.

### Worked Example:
The average of 5 consecutive odd numbers is 25. Find the largest.
*   **Middle term:** 25.
*   **Sequence:** 21, 23, [25], 27, 29.
*   **Largest:** 29.
