# Averages - Practice Beginner

**Q1.** Find the average of first 50 natural numbers.
> 🎯 Hint: Average $= (N + 1) / 2$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** Average $= (50 + 1) / 2 = 25.5$.
*   **Answer:** 25.5
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐☆☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 15s
</details>
