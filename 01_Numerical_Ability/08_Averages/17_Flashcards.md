# Averages - Flashcards

*   **Card 1: What is the average of first N natural numbers?**
    *   *Answer:* $\frac{N+1}{2}$.
*   **Card 2: What is the average of first N even numbers?**
    *   *Answer:* $N+1$.
*   **Card 3: What is the average of consecutive numbers in AP?**
    *   *Answer:* The middle term (or the average of the first and last terms).
*   **Card 4: How does adding a constant $C$ to all observations affect the mean?**
    *   *Answer:* The mean increases by exactly $C$.
