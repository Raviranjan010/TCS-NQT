# Permutation & Combination - Flashcards

*   **Card 1: When do we use combinations instead of permutations?**
    *   *Answer:* When selecting a group where the internal order of items does not matter (e.g., choosing committee members).
*   **Card 2: What is the value of 0! ?**
    *   *Answer:* 1.
*   **Card 3: How to arrange repeating items?**
    *   *Answer:* Divide the total factorial by the product of factorials of individual repeating occurrences: $\frac{n!}{p! q!}$.
*   **Card 4: How to arrange items in a circle?**
    *   *Answer:* Fix one item in place, then arrange the remaining $(n-1)$ items linearly in $(n-1)!$ ways.
