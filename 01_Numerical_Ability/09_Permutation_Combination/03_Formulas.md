# Permutation & Combination - Formulas & Derivations

### 1. Circular Permutations
$$(n-1)!$$
*   **Derivation:** When arranging $n$ people in a circle, we must fix the position of 1 person to break the rotational symmetry. The remaining $n-1$ people are then arranged linearly in $(n-1)!$ ways.
*   **Worked Example:** Arrange 6 people at a round table.
    $$\text{Ways} = (6-1)! = 5! = 120\text{ ways}$$
