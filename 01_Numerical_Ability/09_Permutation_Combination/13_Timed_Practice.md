# Permutation & Combination - Timed Practice

**Q1.** Find the number of words that can be formed using all letters of "MATHEMATICS".
> 🎯 Hint: Count letters and check repeats (M=2, A=2, T=2).
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** Total letters $= 11$. Repeats: M=2, A=2, T=2.
    $$\text{Total Ways} = \frac{11!}{2! 2! 2!} = 4,989,600$$
*   **Answer:** 4,989,600
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 45s
</details>
