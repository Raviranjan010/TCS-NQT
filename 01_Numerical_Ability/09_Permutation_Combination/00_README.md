# 09 Permutation & Combination Directory Overview

Welcome to the **Permutations & Combinations (P&C)** module of the TCS NQT study guide. This directory is designed to build foundational clarity, formula derivation knowledge, fast mental shortcut strategies, and pattern recognition skills to tackle counting problems under intense exam pressure.

---

## 📊 Module Weightage & Exam Relevance
*   **Section**: Section 1A (Numerical Ability) & Section 1C (Advanced Quant & Reasoning)
*   **Average Questions**: 2–3 Questions (highly prone to being non-MCQ Fill-in-the-Blank type)
*   **Target Time**: Under 75 seconds per question
*   **Prerequisites**: Basic arithmetic multiplication, division, and factorial simplification.
