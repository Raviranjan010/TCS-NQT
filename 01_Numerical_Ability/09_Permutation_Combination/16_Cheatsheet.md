# Permutation & Combination - Cheatsheet

*   **Permutation Formula:** $^nP_r = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!}$
*   **Combination Formula:** $^nC_r = \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}$
*   **Circular Permutation:** $P_{circ} = (n-1)!$
*   **Necklace Circular:** $P_{neck} = \frac{(n-1)!}{2}$
*   **Polygon Diagonals:** $D = \frac{n(n-3)}{2}$
*   **Identical Objects Arrangement:** $\frac{n!}{p! q! r!}$
