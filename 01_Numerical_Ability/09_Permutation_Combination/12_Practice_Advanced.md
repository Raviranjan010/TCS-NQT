# Permutation & Combination - Practice Advanced

**Q1.** In how many ways can 4 boys and 4 girls be seated at a round table so that no two girls sit together?
> 🎯 Hint: Seat the boys first, then place the girls in the gaps.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:**
    *   Arrange 4 boys in a circle: $(4-1)! = 3! = 6$ ways.
    *   This creates 4 gaps. Arrange 4 girls in these 4 gaps: $4! = 24$ ways.
    *   Total ways $= 6 \times 24 = 144$.
*   **Answer:** 144
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ | **Target Time:** 60s
</details>
