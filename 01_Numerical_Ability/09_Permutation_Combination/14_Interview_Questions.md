# Permutation & Combination - Interview Questions

**Q1.** How do permutations help in understanding key space calculations in cryptography?
*   **Answer:** The strength of an encryption key is determined by the size of the key space. If a key is formed by $N$ bits, the number of permutations is $2^N$. In brute-force attacks, the search space complexity grows exponentially, demonstrating why larger keys are cryptographically secure.
