# Permutation & Combination - Practice Intermediate

**Q1.** How many diagonals can be drawn in a decagon (10-sided polygon)?
> 🎯 Hint: Diagonals $= ^nC_2 - n$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:**
    $$\text{Diagonals} = ^{10}C_2 - 10 = 45 - 10 = 35$$
*   **Answer:** 35
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 30s
</details>
