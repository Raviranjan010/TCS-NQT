# 09 Permutation Combination - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

---

**Q1.** In how many different ways can the letters of a word containing 3 vowels and 5 consonants be arranged so that the vowels are always together?
*   **Type:** Arrangement with Constraints
*   **Answer:** Group all vowels into a single unit. Total units = 5 consonants + 1 group = 6 units. These can be arranged in (6)! ways. Within the group, vowels can be arranged in 3! ways. Total arrangements = 720 * 6 = 4320 ways.
*   **Time target:** 50 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** Medium (appears in 50% of papers)
