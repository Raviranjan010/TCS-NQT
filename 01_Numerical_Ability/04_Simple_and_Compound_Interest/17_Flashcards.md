# Simple and Compound Interest - Flashcards

*   **Card 1: What is the principal in compound interest?**
    *   *Answer:* The principal changes each compounding period to include the accumulated interest of all previous periods.
*   **Card 2: If interest is compounded half-yearly, how do rate and time adjust?**
    *   *Answer:* The annual interest rate is divided by 2, and the time period (in years) is multiplied by 2.
*   **Card 3: What is the relation between 2-year and 3-year differences of CI and SI?**
    *   *Answer:* $\text{Diff}_3 = \text{Diff}_2 \times (3 + R/100)$.
*   **Card 4: How does depreciation rate affect value over time?**
    *   *Answer:* The value decreases exponentially, calculated by $P(1 - R/100)^T$.
