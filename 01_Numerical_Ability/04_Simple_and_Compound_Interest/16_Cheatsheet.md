# Simple and Compound Interest - Cheatsheet

*   **Compounding Cycles:**
    *   Half-Yearly: $R' = R/2$, $T' = 2T$
    *   Quarterly: $R' = R/4$, $T' = 4T$
*   **Depreciation Formula:**
    *   $V_T = P\left(1 - \frac{R}{100}\right)^T$
*   **Installments for SI:**
    *   Annual Installment $= \frac{100A}{100T + \frac{RT(T-1)}{2}}$
*   **Installments for CI (2 Years):**
    *   Principal $P = \frac{X}{1 + R/100} + \frac{X}{(1 + R/100)^2}$ where $X$ is equal installment.
