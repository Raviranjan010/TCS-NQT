# Simple and Compound Interest - Shortcuts & Speed Hacks

## ⚡ 1. Rule of 72 (Doubling Time at CI)
*   **Shortcut:** $\text{Time to double} \approx \frac{72}{\text{Rate of interest}}$
*   **Real Number Demo:** At $8\%$ p.a. CI, money doubles in $\frac{72}{8} = 9$ years.

## ⚡ 2. Effective Rate of Interest for CI (2 Years)
*   **Shortcut:** $\text{Effective Rate} = 2R + \frac{R^2}{100}\%$
*   **Real Number Demo:** If $R = 6\%$ p.a., the effective 2-year compounded rate is $2(6) + \frac{36}{100} = 12.36\%$.
