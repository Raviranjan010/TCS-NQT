# Simple and Compound Interest - Expected Questions

### Question 1. Three-year interest difference
*   **Question:** Find the difference between CI and SI on ₹10,000 for 3 years at $10\%$ p.a.
*   **Solution:**
    $$\text{Diff}_3 = 10000 \times \left(\frac{10}{100}\right)^2 \times \left(3 + \frac{10}{100}\right) = 10000 \times 0.01 \times 3.1 = ₹310$$
