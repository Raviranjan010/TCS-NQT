# 04 Simple and Compound Interest - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

---

**Q1.** Find the difference between compound interest and simple interest on a sum of Rs.2000 at 6% per annum for 2 years.
*   **Type:** Difference between CI and SI
*   **Answer:** Difference = P * (R/100)^2 = 2000 * (6/100)^2 = 7.2
*   **Time target:** 50 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 80% of papers)
