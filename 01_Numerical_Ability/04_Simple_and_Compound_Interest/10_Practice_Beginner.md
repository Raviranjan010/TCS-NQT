# Simple and Compound Interest - Practice Beginner

**Q1.** Find the SI on ₹5,000 at $12\%$ per annum for 2 years.
> 🎯 Hint: Use the basic SI formula $SI = P \cdot R \cdot T / 100$.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Approach:** Formula-based.
*   **Step-by-step:** $P=5000, R=12, T=2$. $SI = 5000 \times 12 \times 2 / 100 = 1200$.
*   **Answer:** ₹1,200
*   **Shortcut:** $12\% \times 2 = 24\%$. $24\% \text{ of } 5000 = 1200$.
*   **Common Mistake:** Forgetting to divide by 100.
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐☆☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 30s
</details>
