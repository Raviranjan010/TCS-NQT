# Simple and Compound Interest - Practice Intermediate

**Q1.** A sum compounded annually becomes ₹1,352 in 2 years and ₹1,406.08 in 3 years. Find the rate of interest.
> 🎯 Hint: The interest earned in the 3rd year is the interest on the amount at the end of the 2nd year.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Approach:** Shifting base for compound interest.
*   **Step-by-step:** Interest in 3rd year $= 1406.08 - 1352 = 54.08$. This is earned on base ₹1352.
    $$\text{Rate} = \frac{54.08}{1352} \times 100 = 4\%$$
*   **Answer:** $4\%$
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | **Target Time:** 60s
</details>
