# Simple and Compound Interest - Pattern Recognition Guide

## 1. The "Times" Growth Pattern at CI
*   **Trigger Word:** *"A sum becomes X times in Y years at Compound Interest. In how many years will it become Z times?"*
*   **Strategy:** If a sum becomes $k$ times in $t$ years, it becomes $k^n$ times in $n \times t$ years.

### Immediate Worked Example:
A sum of money doubles itself in 5 years at CI. In how many years will it become 8 times itself?
*   **Setup:** $2^1$ times in 5 years.
*   **Target:** $8 = 2^3$ times.
*   **Calculation:** $3 \times 5 = 15$ years.
