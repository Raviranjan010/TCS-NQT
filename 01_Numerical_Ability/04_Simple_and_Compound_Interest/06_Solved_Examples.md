# Simple and Compound Interest - Solved Examples

## 🟢 Easy-Level Examples
### Example 1. Basic SI
*   **Question:** A sum of ₹4,000 is lent at $5\%$ SI for 2 years. Find the interest.
*   **Solution:**
    $$\text{SI} = \frac{4000 \times 5 \times 2}{100} = ₹400$$

## 🟡 Medium-Level Examples
### Example 2. Difference between CI & SI
*   **Question:** The difference between CI and SI on a sum for 2 years at $8\%$ is ₹64. Find the principal.
*   **Solution:**
    $$\text{Diff} = P\left(\frac{R}{100}ight)^2 \implies 64 = P \left(\frac{8}{100}\right)^2 \implies 64 = P \times \frac{64}{10000} \implies P = ₹10,000$$

## 🔴 Hard-Level Examples
### Example 3. CI Compounding Installments
*   **Question:** A loan of ₹10,250 is to be paid back in two equal annual installments at $5\%$ compound interest. Find the value of each installment.
*   **Solution:** Let installment be $X$.
    $$P = \frac{X}{1.05} + \frac{X}{(1.05)^2} \implies 10250 = X \left(\frac{20}{21} + \frac{400}{441}\right)$$
    $$10250 = X \left(\frac{420 + 400}{441}\right) = X \left(\frac{820}{441}\right) \implies X = \frac{10250 \times 441}{820} = ₹5,512.50$$
