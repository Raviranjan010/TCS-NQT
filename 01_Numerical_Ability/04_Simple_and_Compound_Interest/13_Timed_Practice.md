# Simple and Compound Interest - Timed Practice

**Q1.** Find the compound interest on ₹25,000 for 1 year at $20\%$ per annum compounded half-yearly.
> 🎯 Hint: Convert rate to $10\%$ and time to 2 half-years.
<details>
<summary>✅ Solution</summary>
*   **Step-by-step:** $R = 10\%, T = 2$. $A = 25000 \times 1.1 \times 1.1 = 30250$. $CI = 30250 - 25000 = 5250$.
*   **Answer:** ₹5,250
*   **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐☆☆☆ | **Target Time:** 45s
</details>
