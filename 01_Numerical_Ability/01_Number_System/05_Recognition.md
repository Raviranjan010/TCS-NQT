# Pattern Recognition: Number System

Identify which mathematical operation to perform based on key exam phrases.

---

## 🔎 Trigger Words to Math Translation

| If the NQT question says... | ...Immediately translate to this operation |
| :--- | :--- |
| "finds the least number which when divided by..." | **LCM (Least Common Multiple)** |
| "finds the greatest length that divides exactly..." | **HCF (Highest Common Factor)** |
| "finds the digit at the ones place for..." | **Unit Digit Exponent Cycle** |
| "how many numbers divide $N$ completely..." | **Total Factors Count** |
| "finds the remainder when $A^B$ is divided by prime $P$..." | **Fermat's Little Theorem** |

---

## 🚦 Difficulty Mapping Curve

- **Easy**: Straight divisibility check of small integers, or unit digit of simple powers (e.g. $17^{40}$).
- **Medium**: Divisors meeting specific conditions (e.g., "factors divisible by 12"), or LCM problems with different remainders.
- **Hard**: Large power remainders requiring Euler's totient theorem, or complex cryptarithmetic addition carries.
