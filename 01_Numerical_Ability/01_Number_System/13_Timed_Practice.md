# Timed Practice: Number System

**Time Limit**: 12 Minutes  
**Questions**: 10  
**Format**: MCQ & FIB

---

## 📝 Part 1: Question Booklet

1. Find the sum of all divisors of $72$. (FIB)
2. What is the unit digit of $17^{2023}$? (MCQ: A. 7, B. 9, C. 3, D. 1)
3. If the 9-digit number $785X3678Y$ is divisible by 72, find $X + Y$. (FIB)
4. Find the number of factors of $180$ that are even. (FIB)
5. Find the least number which when divided by 12, 16, and 18 leaves remainder 5 in each case. (FIB)
6. Find the HCF of $4/5$ and $6/25$. (MCQ: A. $2/25$, B. $12/5$, C. $2/5$, D. $12/25$)
7. Vikram spots a thief at $100\text{m}$. Police runs at $8\text{ m/s}$, thief at $6\text{ m/s}$. Find catch-up time. (FIB)
8. Find the number of proper factors of $120$. (FIB)
9. In cryptarithmetic, if $SEND + MORE = MONEY$, what does $M$ represent? (FIB)
10. If $a \equiv 3 \pmod 5$, find $a^2 \pmod 5$. (FIB)

---

## 🔑 Part 2: Answer Key & Solutions

1. **Answer**: 195.
   - Solution: $72 = 2^3 \times 3^2 \implies (1+2+4+8)(1+3+9) = 15 \times 13 = 195$.
2. **Answer**: C (3).
   - Solution: $2023 \pmod 4 = 3 \implies 7^3 = 343 \implies 3$.
3. **Answer**: 10.
   - Solution: last 3 digits $78Y \implies 784/8 = 98 \implies Y = 4$. Sum $= 48 + X \implies X = 6$. $X+Y = 10$.
4. **Answer**: 12.
   - Solution: $180 = 2^2 \times 3^2 \times 5^1$. Even factors $= 2 \times (2+1)(1+1) = 2 \times 3 \times 2 = 12$.
5. **Answer**: 149.
   - Solution: $\text{LCM}(12, 16, 18) = 144$. Number $= 144 + 5 = 149$.
6. **Answer**: A ($2/25$).
   - Solution: $\frac{\text{HCF}(4,6)}{\text{LCM}(5,25)} = 2/25$.
7. **Answer**: 50.
   - Solution: catch-up rate $= 8-6=2\text{ m/s}$. Time $= 100/2=50\text{ s}$.
8. **Answer**: 15.
   - Solution: $120 = 2^3 \times 3^1 \times 5^1 \implies \text{Total} = 16$. Proper $= 15$.
9. **Answer**: 1.
   - Solution: Standard cryptarithmetic assignment carry $= 1$.
10. **Answer**: 4.
    - Solution: $a^2 \equiv 3^2 \equiv 9 \equiv 4 \pmod 5$.
