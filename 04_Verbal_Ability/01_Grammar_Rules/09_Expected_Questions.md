# 01 Grammar Rules - Expected Questions

This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

**Q1.** Identify the grammatically incorrect sentence: A) Neither of the candidates are ready. B) She works hard. C) Each boy has a laptop. D) Everyone agrees.
*   **Type:** Subject-Verb Agreement Identification
*   **Answer:** Sentence A is incorrect. 'Neither' is a singular indefinite pronoun and requires a singular verb: 'Neither of the candidates is ready'.
*   **Time target:** 25 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 80% of papers)
