**Q1.** Find the error in the sentence: 'Hardly had he finished compiling than the system crashed.'
*   **Type:** Correlative Conjunctions Error Detection
*   **Answer:** The error is the word 'than'. 'Hardly' must be paired with 'when' instead of 'than'. Correct: 'Hardly had he finished compiling when the system crashed.'
*   **Time target:** 30 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 80% of papers)
