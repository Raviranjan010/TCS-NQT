**Q1.** Find the distance between the points A(3, 4) and B(-1, 7) on the Cartesian plane.
*   **Type:** Distance Formula
*   **Answer:** Distance = sqrt((x2 - x1)^2 + (y2 - y1)^2) = sqrt((-1 - 3)^2 + (7 - 4)^2) = sqrt((-4)^2 + 3^2) = sqrt(16 + 9) = 5 units.
*   **Time target:** 35 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** Medium (appears in 50% of papers)
