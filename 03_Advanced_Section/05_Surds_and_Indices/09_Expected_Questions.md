This file contains 15 TCS NQT-style expected questions based on 2022-2025 patterns.

**Q1.** Simplify the expression: (2^n * 4^(2n-1)) / 8^n.
*   **Type:** Laws of Indices
*   **Answer:** Express as base 2: (2^n * 2^(4n-2)) / 2^(3n) = 2^(n + 4n - 2 - 3n) = 2^(2n - 2).
*   **Time target:** 40 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 65% of papers)
