**Q1.** In the addition SEND + MORE = MONEY, find the unique digit value associated with the letter 'M'.
*   **Type:** Classic Cryptarithmetic Addition
*   **Answer:** In a sum of two 4-digit numbers resulting in a 5-digit number, the carry-over digit must be 1. Therefore, M = 1.
*   **Time target:** 90 seconds
*   **TCS frequency:** High (appears in 70% of papers)
