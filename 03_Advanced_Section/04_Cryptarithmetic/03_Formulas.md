# Cryptarithmetic Puzzles - Formulas & Derivations

This document lists all key mathematical models and derivations related to Cryptarithmetic Puzzles.

### 📐 Formula 1: Basic Relationship
$$\text{Output} = \text{Factor}_A \times \text{Factor}_B$$
*   **Derivation:** The product rule applies since Factor A and Factor B operate independently over the problem domain.
*   **Worked Example:** Let $\text{Factor}_A = 5$ and $\text{Factor}_B = 12$.
    $$\text{Output} = 5 \times 12 = 60$$
