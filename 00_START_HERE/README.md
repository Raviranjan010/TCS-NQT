## 📊 TCS NQT 2026 Exam Pattern
The TCS NQT is a sequential, adaptive, or time-locked test. You cannot navigate back to a previous question once submitted.

## 🎯 Sectional Attempt & Scoring Strategy
To maximize your hiring bracket, prioritize your focus using this target sequence:

1.  **Verbal & Reasoning (Ninja/Digital Anchors)**: These sections have the lowest difficulty. Secure these marks first to guarantee your baseline Ninja offer.
2.  **Numerical Ability**: Focus on high-ROI topics (Percentages, Profit & Loss, Averages) to push into the Digital bracket.
3.  **Advanced Sections & Coding (Prime Anchors)**: Spend your maximum mental energy on the Easy and Medium coding problems. Passing the private test cases here is the single biggest factor for a Prime recommendation.
