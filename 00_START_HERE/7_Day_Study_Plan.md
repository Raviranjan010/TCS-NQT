## 📝 Day-by-Day Targets

### 🎯 Day 1: Percentages & Data Interpretation
*   **Folder Focus**: [02_Percentage](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/02_Percentage) & [11_Data_Interpretation](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/11_Data_Interpretation)
*   **Concepts**: Fraction-percentage equivalents, multiplying factors, and product constancy.
*   **Practice**: Solve Solved Examples 1 to 7, and Beginner Practice 1 to 5.
*   **Pre-Sleep Revision**: Memorize the fraction conversion scale (e.g., $1/7 = 14.28\%$, $1/8 = 12.5\%$).

### 🎯 Day 2: Profit & Loss, Averages, & Mixtures
*   **Folder Focus**: [03_Profit_and_Loss](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/03_Profit_and_Loss) & [08_Averages](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/08_Averages)
*   **Concepts**: Successive markups/discounts, average speed calculations, and mixture alligation weights.
*   **Practice**: Solve Profit & Loss Solved Examples 1 to 10. Attempt the Timed Practice.
*   **Pre-Sleep Revision**: Review the shortcut for equal percentage swings: $\text{Net Loss} = (P/10)^2\%$.

### 🎯 Day 3: Seating Arrangements & Logical Syllogisms
*   **Folder Focus**: [02_Reasoning_Ability](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/02_Reasoning_Ability)
*   **Concepts**: Linear seating, circular seating constraints, and syllogism Venn Diagram relations.
*   **Practice**: Solve 10 seating arrangement puzzles. Take the Reasoning Mini-Mock.
*   **Pre-Sleep Revision**: Review the circular alternate seating formula: $(M-1)! \times W!$.

### 🎯 Day 4: Advanced Quant (P&C & Probability)
*   **Folder Focus**: [09_Permutation_Combination](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/09_Permutation_Combination) & [10_Probability](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/10_Probability)
*   **Concepts**: Permutation vs Combination, duplicate arrangements, circular table seating, Beggar's method, and grid paths.
*   **Practice**: Read [09_Permutation_Combination/01_Concept.md](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/09_Permutation_Combination/01_Concept.md) and [04_Shortcuts.md](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/09_Permutation_Combination/04_Shortcuts.md). Solve all 10 [Solved Examples](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/09_Permutation_Combination/06_Solved_Examples.md).
*   **Pre-Sleep Revision**: Run through the Dictionary Rank cross-out algorithm and the Star & Bars boundary cases.

### 🎯 Day 5: Verbal Ability & SQL CS Basics
*   **Folder Focus**: [04_Verbal_Ability](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/04_Verbal_Ability) & [06_Technical_CS](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/06_Technical_CS)
*   **Concepts**: Sentence completion (grammar/prepositions), Email templates, and SQL JOINs.
*   **Practice**: Write out the 120-word email template once by hand. Solve 15 sentence completion sentences. Practice 5 SQL query examples.
*   **Pre-Sleep Revision**: Review the sequencing constraints for email writing (outline word list must be used in exact order).

### 🎯 Day 6: Coding Foundations (Easy & Medium DSA)
*   **Folder Focus**: [05_Coding](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/05_Coding)
*   **Concepts**: Array rotation, string reversals, and backtracking (unique permutations/combinations).
*   **Practice**: Open [09_Permutation_Combination/14_Interview_Questions.md](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/09_Permutation_Combination/14_Interview_Questions.md). Type out, compile, and dry-run both C++ templates.
*   **Pre-Sleep Revision**: Verify time complexity classes (e.g. $O(N \times N!)$ for permutations, $O(k \times ^nC_k)$ for combinations).

### 🎯 Day 7: Timed Mock Tests & Final Night-Before Revision
*   **Folder Focus**: [07_Mock_Tests](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/07_Mock_Tests) & [09_Revision](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/09_Revision)
*   **Concepts**: Full-length exam simulation.
*   **Practice**:
    *   **Morning**: Take Mock Test 1 (Numerical + Reasoning, 50 mins). Review all errors.
    *   **Afternoon**: Take Mock Test 2 (Verbal + Coding, 116 mins). Debug all private test cases.
    *   **Night**: Open [16_Cheatsheet.md](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/09_Permutation_Combination/16_Cheatsheet.md). Review the top 3 traps, decision trees, and memory tricks for all topics.
*   **Pre-Sleep Revision**: Sleep early. Do not cram new topics.
