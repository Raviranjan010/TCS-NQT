# TCS NQT 2-Day Crash Plan

If you have exactly 48 hours left before your exam, this hour-by-hour crash plan is designed to help you extract the maximum possible marks by focusing strictly on high-yield cheat sheets and solved examples.

Your goal on Day 1 is to secure the basic Foundation section cutoff, qualifying you for the Ninja hiring bracket.

```

         +----------------------------+----------------------------+
         | Hours 0 - 4                | Hours 4 - 8                | Hours 8 - 12
         v                            v                            v
     CORE ARITHMETIC              LOGICAL REASONING            VERBAL & ADVANCED QUANT
     Percentages, P&L.            Seating, Syllogisms.         AP/GP, P&C shortcuts,
     Read Solved Examples.        Read Visualizations.         Email templates.
```

### Hour-by-Hour Breakdown

*   **Hour 0 – Hour 2: Percentages & Fractions (Arithmetic Base)**
    *   *Action*: Open [02_Percentage/16_Cheatsheet.md](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/02_Percentage/16_Cheatsheet.md) and [02_Percentage/06_Solved_Examples.md](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/02_Percentage/06_Solved_Examples.md).
    *   *Task*: Solve Solved Examples 1, 4, 6, and 8. Master multiplying factors.
    *   *Skip*: Skip beginner/advanced practice sets completely.
*   **Hour 2 – Hour 4: Profit & Loss and Averages**
    *   *Action*: Open Profit & Loss cheatsheet and Solved Examples.
    *   *Task*: Practice markup, successive discounts, and average speed mixtures.
*   **Hour 4 – Hour 6: Seating Arrangements (Logical Anchor)**
    *   *Action*: Open Reasoning Seating Arrangements visualization files.
    *   *Task*: Study circular and linear placing rules. Practice placing adjacent constraints.
*   **Hour 6 – Hour 8: Syllogisms & Blood Relations**

*   **Hour 8 – Hour 10: Sentence Completion & Email Writing (Verbal)**
    *   *Action*: Open Verbal Ability guides for Cloze tests and Email Writing.
    *   *Task*: Memorize the standard 120-word professional email template and the rule for sequencing outline words.
*   **Hour 10 – Hour 12: Advanced Quant Shortcuts (P&C & Probability)**
    *   *Action*: Open [09_Permutation_Combination/04_Shortcuts.md](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/09_Permutation_Combination/04_Shortcuts.md) and [09_Permutation_Combination/08_PYQs.md](file:///d:/Temp/TCS-NQT/01_Numerical_Ability/09_Permutation_Combination/08_PYQs.md).
