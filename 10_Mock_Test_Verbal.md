## 📧 Section 4: Email Writing (Q24)

### Q24 (9 Minutes)
* **Context**: Write a formal email to a customer explaining that their order delivery will be delayed due to severe weather conditions.
* **Outline Words**: `order number` $\to$ `transit` $\to$ `heavy snowfall` $\to$ `safety reasons` $\to$ `expected arrival` $\to$ `sincerely apologize`

---

## 🔑 Answers & Model Responses

### Section 1: Sentence Completion
1. **(B) perturbed** — Perturbed means anxious or unsettled. The negative effect of cancelling holidays aligns with this.
2. **(A) from** — The verb *abstain* is always followed by the preposition *from*.
3. **(B) Each of the students has completed his or her assignment on time.** — "Each" is singular and takes the singular verb "has" and singular pronoun "his or her".
4. **(A) engaging, tedious** — "While" indicates contrast: positive *engaging* versus negative *tedious*.
5. **(B) had left** — Third conditional: If + Past Perfect (had left) $\to$ would have + past participle (would have missed).
6. **(B) with** — The adjective *compatible* pairs with *with*.
7. **(B) irrelevant** — If the evidence is dismissed, it must be irrelevant.
8. **(B) scanty, reduction** — Scanty (sparse) rainfall leads to a reduction in yields.
9. **(B) Optional** — Opposite of mandatory (compulsory/essential) is optional.
10. **(C) was present** — In "Neither/nor", the verb agrees with the closer subject "teachers" (plural), so it must be "were present".
11. **(B) until** — Defer means postpone, which aligns with *until* a future date.
12. **(A) Not only did he write the script, but he also directed the play.** — Correct parallel inversion.
13. **(B) revolutionary** — Revolutionary findings are those that challenge long-standing theories.
14. **(B) to** — *Sensitive* is followed by the preposition *to*.
15. **(C) than watching** — *Prefer* takes *to* (prefer X to Y), so it should be "to watching".
16. **(B) sluggish, complaining** — Sluggish (slow) service causes clients to complain.
17. **(B) succinct** — Succinct means brief and clear, fitting the "under two minutes" detail.
18. **(B) will have completed** — Future perfect (will have completed) is used for actions finished by a certain future time (by this time next year).
19. **(B) to** — *Adapt* is followed by the preposition *to*.
20. **(B) scarcity, before** — Scarcity of resources (negative) contrasted with delivering before the deadline (positive).

### Section 2: Instruction Passage
21. **(A) Half a day's salary deduction.** — Rohan has 4 late arrivals. 3 late arrivals trigger the penalty of half a day's casual leave deduction. Since Rohan has zero casual leaves left, the penalty converts to half a day's salary deduction. (The 4th late arrival does not trigger a second penalty since penalties are grouped by threes).

### Section 3: Passage Recall Model Responses
* **Q22 Model Recall**: Polar ice caps are melting by nine percent every decade, leading to rising sea levels that endanger cities like Miami and Venice. Scientists caution that a forty percent reduction in greenhouse gas emissions is required by 2030 to prevent severe flooding.
* **Q23 Model Recall**: Although generative AI tools have boosted copywriting efficiency by fifty percent, editors express concern over the reduction in original narrative quality. Currently, seventy percent of publishers rely on AI for drafting material.

### Section 4: Email Writing Model Response
* **Q24 Model Email (115 words)**:
  > **Subject**: Delivery Update on Your Order
  >
  > Dear Valued Customer,
  >
  > I am writing to update you regarding your recent purchase with **order number** 48201. Your package is currently in **transit**, but we have encountered unexpected delays on the shipping route.
  >
  > Due to **heavy snowfall** across the northern transit corridor, several highways have been closed. For **safety reasons**, our logistics partner has temporarily suspended truck movement in those regions. The **expected arrival** of your package has been rescheduled to Friday, June 29th.
  >
  > We **sincerely apologize** for this delay and any inconvenience it may cause. We appreciate your patience as we work to deliver your order safely.
  >
  > Best regards,  
  > Customer Support Team
